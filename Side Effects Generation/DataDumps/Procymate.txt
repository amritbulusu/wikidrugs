'''Procymate''' ('''Equipax''') is a carbamate derivative which is a [[sedative]] and [[anxiolytic]] drug. It was previously manufactured and marketed in [[Belgium]]. The usual dose for adults is 800 to 1200 mg per day.<ref>"Procymate," ''Bank of Automated Data on Drugs.'' VIDAL Drug Database. <http://www.biam2.org/www/Sub394.html> </ref> Procymate shares a similar structure and [[mechanism of action]] with the related drugs [[hexapropymate]] and [[phenprobamate]].
