'''5-DBFPV ''' (also known as '''5-dihydrobenzofuranpyrovalerone''' and '''3-desoxy-MDPV''') is a [[stimulant]] of the [[substituted cathinone|cathinone]] class that has been sold online as a [[designer drug]].<ref>{{cite web | url=https://www.caymanchem.com/Product.vm/catalog/16437 | title=5-DBFPV | publisher=Cayman Chemical}}</ref><ref name="Mitsumoto_2015">{{cite (25I-NBMD, RH34, and escaline), eight cathinone derivatives, and a phencyclidine analog MMXE, newly identified in ingredients of drug products before they were sold on the drug market|url=https://link.springer.com/article/10.1007/s11419-015-0293-6|journal=Forensic Toxicology|date=January == Legal status ==
5-DBFPV is illegal in Sweden as of 26. January 2016.<ref>{{cite web | url=https://www.folkhalsomyndigheten.se/nyheter-och-press/nyhetsarkiv/2015/november/31-nya-amnen-kan-klassas-som-narkotika-eller-halsofarlig-vara/ | title=31 nya ämnen kan klassas som narkotika eller hälsofarlig vara | publisher=Folkhälsomyndigheten | language=Swedish | date=November 2015}}</ref>
