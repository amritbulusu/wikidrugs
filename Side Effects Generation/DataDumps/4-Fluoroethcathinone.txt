'''4-Fluoroethcathinone''' (also known as '''4-FEC''') is a stimulant of the [[substituted cathinone|cathinone]] class and [[structural analog]] of [[flephedrone]] (4-fluoromethcathinone) that has been sold as [[designer drug]].<ref>{{cite web | url=https://www.caymanchem.com/product/11231 | title=4-Fluoroethcathinone (hydrochloride) | publisher=Cayman Chemical}}</ref><ref>{{cite journal|first1=Folker|last1=Westphal|first2=Thomas|last2=Junge|title=Ring positional differentiation of isomeric N-alkylated fluorocathinones by gas chromatography/tandem mass spectrometry|url=http://www.sciencedirect.com/science/article/pii/S037907381200374X|journal=Forensic Science International|date=30 November 2012|pages=97–105|volume=223|issue=1–3|doi=10.1016/j.forsciint.2012.08.011|pmid=22940190}}</ref>
