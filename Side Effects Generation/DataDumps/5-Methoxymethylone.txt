'''5-Methoxy-Methylone''' (also known as '''2-A1MP''' and '''βk-MMDMA''') is a [[chemical compound]] of the [[substituted cathinone|cathinone]] class which has been sold online as a [[designer drug]]. It is the beta-ketone version of [[MMDMA (drug)|5-Methoxy-MDMA]].<ref>{{cite web |url=https://www.caymanchem.com/app/template/Product.vm/catalog/9002195 |title=5-Methoxy-Methylone (aka 2-a1mp/2-aimp) |author=CaymanChemical |accessdate=October 19, 2015}}</ref><ref>{{cite web |url=https://www.caymanchem.com/gcms/9002195-0466680-GCMS.pdf |title=5-Methoxy-Methylone GC/MS data |author=CaymanChemical |accessdate=October 19, 2015}}</ref><ref>{{cite web |url=https://www.caymanchem.com/msdss/9002195m.pdf |title=5-Methoxy-Methylone MSDS|author=CaymanChemical |accessdate=October 19, 2015}}</ref> The more common name 2-AIMP/2-A1MP does not appear to relate to the molecular structure. In comparison with [[methylone]], the two compounds differ by the addition of a [[methoxy]] group at the 5th carbon atom of the aromatic ring. The toxicity of this compound is unknown.

==Legal Status==
5-Methoxy-Methylone is listed as an illegal drug under the name 2-A1MP in Hungary.<ref>[http://www.daath.hu/incoming/designer_jogi_lista_20150903_BSZKI_Daath_kieg.pdf A Magyarországon megjelent, a Kábítószer és Kábítószer-függőség Európai Megfigyelő Központjának Korai Jelzőrendszerébe (EMCDDA EWS) 2005 óta bejelentett ellenőrzött anyagok büntetőjogi vonatkozású besorolása]</ref>
