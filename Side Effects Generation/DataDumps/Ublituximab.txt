'''Ublituximab''' is an immunomodulator. It binds to [[CD20]].<ref>{{cite journal | author = [[World Health Organization]] | title = International Nonproprietary Names for Pharmaceutical Substances (INN). Proposed INN: List 104 | journal = WHO Drug Information | volume = 24 | issue = 4 | year = 2010 | url = http://www.who.int/medicines/publications/druginformation/innlists/Final_PL104.pdf | format=PDF}}</ref>

It is currently in [[phase 2]] trials for [[multiple sclerosis]].<ref>https://clinicaltrials.gov/ct2/show/NCT02738775</ref>
