'''Aceglatone''' ('''Glucaron''') is an [[antineoplastic]] drug available in Japan.<ref>[http://www.drugs.com/international/glucaron.html Glucaron], drugs.com</ref>

It is an inhibitor of the [[enzyme]] [[Beta-glucuronidase|β-glucuronidase]].<ref>{{cite journal |author1=Iida, Ryohei |author2=Nagata, Sachiko |author3=Kakimoto, Morio |author4=Akaike, Hiroaki |author5=Watanabe, Hiroko |author6=Shioya, Akitoshi | title = 2,5-Di-O-acetyl-D-glucosaccharo-(1→4)(6→3)-dilactone, a new potent β-glucuronidase inhibitor | journal = Japanese Journal of Pharmacology  | year = 1965 | volume = 15 | issue = 1 | pages = 88–90 | doi = 10.1254/jjp.15.88}}</ref>
