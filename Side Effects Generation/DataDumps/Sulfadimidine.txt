'''Sulfadimidine''' or '''sulfamethazine''' is a [[sulfonamide (medicine)|sulfonamide]] [[antibacterial]].

==Availability==
In [[Pakistan]], it is available with the brand name of "Sanadine-33.3 %" as potential treatment of [[E-coli]] enteritis in veterinary medicine. {{Ref|reference_name_A|[9]}}
