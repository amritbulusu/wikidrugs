'''Brioschi''' is the surname of the following people:
* [[Antonio Brioschi]] (fl. c.1725&ndash;1750), Italian composer, pioneer in symphonic music of the early Classical period
* [[Francesco Brioschi]] (1824&ndash;1897), Italian mathematician
* [[Renato Brioschi]] (born 1948), Italian singer and composer

'''Brioschi''' may also refer to:
* [[Brioschi (company)|Brioschi]], Italian medical company
* [[Gaussian_curvature#Alternative_formulas|Brioschi formula]], a formula introduced by Francesco Brioschi for [[Gaussian curvature]]
