'''Sorbitan tristearate''' is a nonionic [[surfactant]]. It is variously used as a dispersing agent, [[emulsifier]], and [[Stabilizer (chemistry)|stabilizer]], [[Food additive|in food]] and in [[aerosol spray]]s.  As a food additive, it has the [[E number]] E492. Brand names for polysorbates include Alkest, Canarcel, and Span. The consistency of sorbitan tristearate is waxy; its color is light cream to tan.
