'''Tioclomarol''' is an [[anticoagulant]] of the [[4-Hydroxycoumarins|4-hydroxycoumarin]] [[vitamin K antagonist]] type. It is a second generation drug, used as a [[rodenticide]] that is effective for the control of rodents that are resistant to this class of drugs.
