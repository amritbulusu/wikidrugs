'''Prednisolamate''', also known as '''prednisolone 21-diethylaminoacetate''', is a [[synthetic compound|synthetic]]  [[corticosteroid]].<ref name="Elks2014">{{cite book|author=J. Elks|title=The Dictionary of Drugs: Chemical Data: Chemical Data, Structures and Bibliographies|url=https://books.google.com/books?id=0vXTBwAAQBAJ&pg=PA1012|date=14 November 2014|publisher=Springer|isbn=978-1-4757-2085-3|pages=1012–}}</ref><ref name="IndexNominum2000">{{cite book|title=Index Nominum 2000: International Drug Directory|url=https://books.google.com/books?id=5GpcTQD_L2oC&pg=PA871|year=2000|publisher=Taylor & Francis|isbn=978-3-88763-075-1|pages=871–}}</ref> It is or was a component of '''Etaproctene''', which contains [[lidocaine]], prednisolamate hydrochloride, and [[tetryzoline]].<ref name="MartindaleSciences1993">{{cite book|author1=William Martindale|author2=Royal Pharmaceutical Society of Great Britain. Dept. of Pharmaceutical Sciences|title=The Extra Pharmacopoeia|url=https://books.google.com/books?id=EGZWAAAAYAAJ|year=1993|publisher=Pharmaceutical Press|isbn=978-0-85369-300-0|page=737}}</ref>
