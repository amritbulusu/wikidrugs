'''Zomebazam''' produced by [[Hoechst AG|Hoechst]] is a pyrazolodiazepinone derivative drug with [[anxiolytic]] properties. It is structurally related to  [[razobazam]] and [[zometapine]].<ref>{{Cite web | url = http://www.psychotropics.dk/moleculeView/default.aspx?ID=1480&Catalogtype=A&ChapterID=237&Thissortorder=2 | title = zomebazam | accessdate = 7 December 2009 | year = 2003 | publisher = psychotropics.dk }}</ref>
