== Legal status ==
Following its sale as a [[designer drug]],<ref>{{cite web | url=http://nsddb.eu/substance/573/ | title=Desmethyletizolam | publisher=New Synthetic Drugs Database}}</ref> metizolam was classified as controlled substance in Sweden on 26. January 2016.<ref>{{cite web | url=https://www.folkhalsomyndigheten.se/nyheter-och-press/nyhetsarkiv/2015/november/31-nya-amnen-kan-klassas-som-narkotika-eller-halsofarlig-vara/ | title=31 nya ämnen kan klassas som narkotika eller hälsofarlig vara | publisher=Folkhälsomyndigheten | language=sv | date=November 2015}}</ref>
