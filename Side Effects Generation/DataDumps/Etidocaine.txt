'''Etidocaine''', marketed under the trade name '''Duranest''', is a [[local anesthetic]] given by [[Injection (medicine)|injection]] during [[surgery|surgical procedures]] and [[childbirth|labor and delivery]]. Etidocaine has a long duration of activity, and the main disadvantage of using during dentistry is increased bleeding during surgery.<ref>{{Cite journal
