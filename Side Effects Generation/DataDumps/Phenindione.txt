'''Phenindione''' is an [[anticoagulant]] which functions as a [[Vitamin K antagonist]].

Phenindione was introduced in the early 1950s. It acts similar to [[warfarin]], but it has been associated with hypersensitivity reactions, so it is rarely used and warfarin is preferred.<ref name="pmid15743920">{{cite journal |title=Characterization of the T-cell response in a patient with phenindione hypersensitivity |journal=J.
