'''Angiotensinamide''' ([[International Nonproprietary Name|INN]]; [[British Approved Name|BAN]] and [[United States Adopted Name|USAN]] '''angiotensin amide''') is a potent [[vasoconstrictor]] used as a [[cardiac]] [[stimulant]]. It is a derivative of [[Angiotensin#Angiotensin II|angiotensin II]].
