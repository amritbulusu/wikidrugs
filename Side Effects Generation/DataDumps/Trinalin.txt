'''Trinalin''' is the trade name of an [[antihistamine]] and [[decongestant]] formulation consisting of one milligram [[azatadine]] [[maleate]] and 120 milligrams of [[pseudoephedrine]] [[sulfate]] in a base of [[acacia]] and other inactive ingredients.<ref>{{Cite web|url=http://www.rxlist.com/cgi/generic/azatpseud.htm|title=Trinalin}}</ref>
