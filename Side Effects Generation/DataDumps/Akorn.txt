'''Akorn Pharmaceuticals''' ({{NASDAQ|AKRX}}) is an American generic pharmaceuticals manufacturer based in [[Lake Forest, Illinois]]. It is a component of the [[NASDAQ Biotechnology Index]].

[[John Kapoor]] is the founder and a major shareholder.<ref name=Forbes>{{cite web|title=John Kapoor|url=https://www.forbes.com/profile/john-kapoor/|website=Forbes|accessdate=12 May 2015}}</ref>

Akorn acquired rival Hi-Tech Pharmacal in 2013.<ref name="reuters">{{cite web|url=https://www.reuters.com/article/us-hitechpharmacal-offer-idUSBRE97Q0EL20130827|author=Reuters Editorial|title=Eye drug maker Akorn to buy rival Hi-Tech Pharmacal for $640 million|website=Reuters|accessdate=2 August 2017}}</ref>

In June 2015, it was reported that a hacker was trying to sell an Akorn customer database of more than 50,000 records. Akorn commented that "Although much of the information acquired is publicly available, we are in the process of notifying our valued customers about this incident".<ref name=CSO>{{cite news|title=Akorn Inc. has customer database stolen, records offered to highest bidder|url=http://www.csoonline.com/article/2938032/data-breach/akorn-inc-has-customer-database-stolen-records-offered-to-highest-bidder.html|accessdate=28 June 2015|work=CSO|date=18 June 2015}}</ref>

In April 2017, Akorn was acquired by [[Fresenius (company)|Fresenius]] for $4.3 billion.<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.bloomberg.com/news/articles/2017-04-24/fresenius-agrees-to-buy-akorn-merck-kgaa-biosimilars-business|title=Fresenius Buys Akorn for $4.3 Billion in U.S. Expansion|author=|date=24 April 2017|publisher=|accessdate=23 September 2017|via=www.bloomberg.com}}</ref>
