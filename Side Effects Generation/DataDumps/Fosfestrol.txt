'''Fosfestrol''' ([[International Nonproprietary Name|INN]], [[British Approved Name|BAN]], [[Japanese Accepted Name|JAN]]) (brand names '''Honvan''', '''Difostilben''', '''Fosfostilben''', '''Fostrolin''', '''Stilbol''', '''Stilphostrol''', '''Vagestrol''', among others), also known as '''diethylstilbestrol diphosphate''' ([[United States Adopted Name|USAN]]), abbreviated as '''DESDP''' or '''DESP''', is a [[Organic compound#Synthetic compounds|synthetic]], [[nonsteroidal]] [[estrogen]] of the [[stilbestrol]] group that is or has been used in the treatment of [[prostate cancer]].<ref name="IndexNominum2000">{{cite book|title=Index Nominum 2000: International Drug Directory|url=https://books.google.com/books?id=5GpcTQD_L2oC&pg=PA332|date=January 2000|publisher=Taylor & Francis|isbn=978-3-88763-075-1|pages=332–}}</ref><ref name="Shanbhag2008">{{cite book|author=Shanbhag|title=Pharmacology: Prep Manual for Undergraduates|url=https://books.google.com/books?id=UJumm_i3M14C&pg=PA463|date=1 January 2008|publisher=Elsevier India|isbn=978-81-312-1153-3|pages=463–}}</ref> It is a [[prodrug]] of [[diethylstilbestrol]].<ref name="Urotext2001">{{cite book|author=Urotext|title=Urotext-Luts: Urology|url=https://books.google.com/books?id=6zjtA37qDsMC&pg=PA386|date=1 January 2001|publisher=Urotext|isbn=978-1-903737-03-3|pages=386–}}</ref> The drug has been marketed widely throughout the world, including in the [[United States]], [[Canada]], [[Europe]], [[Hong Kong]], and [[Mexico]].<ref name="IndexNominum2000" />
