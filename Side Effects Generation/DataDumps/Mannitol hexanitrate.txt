'''Mannitol hexanitrate''' is a powerful [[explosive]].  Physically, it is a powdery solid at normal temperature ranges, with density of 1.73&nbsp;g/cm<sup>3</sup>. The chemical name is hexanitromannitol and it is also known as '''nitromannite''', '''MHN''', and '''nitromannitol''', and by the trademarks '''Nitranitol''' and '''Mannitrin'''. It is more stable than [[nitroglycerin]], and it is used in [[detonator]]s.

Mannitol hexanitrate is a secondary explosive formed by the [[nitration]] of [[mannitol]], a [[sugar alcohol]]. The product is used in medicine as a [[vasodilator]] and as an explosive in blasting caps. Its sensitivity is high, particularly at high temperatures (>&nbsp;75&nbsp;Â°C) where it is slightly more sensitive than nitroglycerine.
