'''Clonitazene''' is an [[opioid]] [[analgesic]] of approximately three times the potency of morphine.{{Citation needed|date=March 2012}} It is related to [[etonitazene]], an opioid of significantly higher potency. Clonitazene is not currently marketed.  It is a controlled substance; in the United States it is a Schedule I Narcotic controlled substance with a DEA ACSCN of 9612 and an annual manufacturing quota of zero.
