'''Maitansine''' ([[International Nonproprietary Name|INN]]), or '''maytansine''' ([[United States Adopted Name|USAN]]), is a [[cytotoxic agent]]. It inhibits the assembly of [[microtubule]]s by binding to [[tubulin]] at the [[rhizoxin]] binding site.<ref name="NCI">[http://www.cancer.gov/Templates/drugdictionary.aspx?CdrID=39492 National Cancer Institute: Definition of Maytansine]</ref>

It is a [[macrolide]] of the [[ansamycin]] type and can be isolated from plants of the genus ''[[Maytenus]]''.<ref name="NCI" />
