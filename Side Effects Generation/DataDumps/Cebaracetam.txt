'''Cebaracetam''' RGPU-95 (p-Cl-Phenylpiracetam) is a chemical in the [[racetam]] family.<ref name="pmid1400764">{{cite journal |author=Chollet D, Künstner P |title=Fast systematic approach for the determination of drugs in biological fluids by fully automated high-performance liquid chromatography with on-line solid-phase extraction and automated cartridge exchange. Application to cebaracetam in human urine |journal=Journal of Chromatography |volume=577 |issue=2 |pages=335–40 |date=June 1992 |pmid=1400764 |doi= 10.1016/0378-4347(92)80255-o|url=}}</ref> It is a [[halogenation|chlorinated]] [[acetyl]][[piperazine]]-substituted [[structural analog|analog]] of [[phenylpiracetam]].
