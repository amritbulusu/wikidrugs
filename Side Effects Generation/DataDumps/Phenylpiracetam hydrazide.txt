'''Phenylpiracetam hydrazide''', also known as '''fonturacetam hydrazide''',<ref>{{cite journal|title=International Nonproprietary Names for Pharmaceutical Substances (INN). Recommended International Nonproprietary Names: List 63|journal=WHO Drug Information|date=2010|volume=24|issue=1|page=56|url=http://apps.who.int/medicinedocs/documents/s17753en/s17753en.pdf|accessdate=26 November 2015}}</ref> is a [[designer drug]] that is a [[derivative (chemistry)|derivative]] of [[phenylpiracetam]] in which the [[carboxamide|amide]] group is replaced with a [[hydrazide]] group.
