'''Theodrenaline''' ([[International Nonproprietary Name|INN]]), also known as '''noradrenalinoethyltheophylline''', is a chemical linkage of [[norepinephrine]] (noradrenaline) and [[theophylline]] used as a [[cardiac stimulant]].
