'''Meglumine''' is an [[amino sugar]] derived from [[glucose]].  It is often used as an [[excipient]] in pharmaceuticals and in conjunction with iodinated compounds in [[contrast medium|contrast media]] such as [[diatrizoate meglumine]], [[iothalamate | iothalamate meglumine]] and [[iodipamide meglumine]].<ref>[http://www.chemicalland21.com/lifescience/phar/N-METHYL-D-GLUCAMINE.htm Meglumine], chemicalland21.com</ref>
