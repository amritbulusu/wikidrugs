'''Phenacaine''', also known as '''holocaine''',<ref>{{cite web | url = http://www.chemicalbook.com/ChemicalProductProperty_EN_CB8407869.htm | title = Holocaine Hydrochloride}}</ref> is a [[local anesthetic]]. It is the only anesthetic other than [[cocaine]] that is approved for ophthalmic use.<ref>''Merck Index'', 1985</ref>
