'''Emepronium''' (as '''emepronium bromide''') is an [[anticholinergic]] drug used in [[urology]] as an [[antispasmodic]].
It can cause ulceration of esophagus, so it should be taken in orthostatic position with sufficient amounts of liquids.<ref>{{cite journal|pmid=3431840|year=1987|last1=Puhakka|first1=HJ|title=Esophageal ulceration due to oral medications|volume=49|issue=6|pages=321â€“6|journal=ORL; journal for oto-rhino-laryngology and its related specialties}}</ref>
