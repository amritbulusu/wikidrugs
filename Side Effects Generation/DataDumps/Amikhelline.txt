'''Amikhelline''' is an [[antimitotic]] drug.<ref>Turchini MF, Geneix A, Perissel B, Malet P, Turchini JP. Characterization of chromosomal aberrations induced in man by various antimitotic agents. (French). ''Comptes Rendus des Seances de la Societe de Biologie et de ses Filiales''. 1985;179(3):331-9. {{PMID|2417673}}</ref> It acts as a [[DNA intercalator]]<ref>Rucheton M, Jeanteur P. Studies on amikhellin. I. Intercalative binding to double-stranded DNA. ''Biochimie''. 1973;55(11):1415-20. {{PMID|4364376}}</ref> and inhibits [[DNA polymerase]].<ref>Rucheton M, Jeanteur P. Studies on amikhellin. II.-Inhibition of dna-polymerase from murine sarcoma leukemia virus. ''Biochimie''. 1976;58(6):689-95. {{PMID|60141}}</ref>
