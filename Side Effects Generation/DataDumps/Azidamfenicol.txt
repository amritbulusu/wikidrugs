'''Azidamfenicol''' is an [[amphenicol]] [[antibiotic]], which has similar profile to [[chloramphenicol]]. It is used only topically, as eye drops and ointment for treatment of susceptible bacterial infections.<ref>Martindale, 35th Edition, 2006</ref>
