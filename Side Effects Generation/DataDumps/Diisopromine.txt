'''Diisopromine''' or '''disoprominum''', usually as the [[hydrochloride]] [[salt (chemistry)|salt]], is a synthetic spasmolytic which neutralizes spastic conditions of the [[biliary tract]] and of the [[sphincter of Oddi]]. It was discovered at [[Janssen Pharmaceutica]] in 1955. It is sold in South Africa under the brand name Agofell syrup as a mixture with [[sorbitol]],<ref>{{cite web|title=AGOFELLÂ® Syrup|url=http://home.intekom.com/pharm/janssen/agofell.html|website=South African electronic package inserts|accessdate=2 September 2015}}</ref> and elsewhere as Megabyl.<ref>{{cite web|title=Guide des medicaments|url=http://www.doctissimo.fr/medicament-MEGABYL.htm|website=Doctissimo|accessdate=2 September 2015}}</ref>
