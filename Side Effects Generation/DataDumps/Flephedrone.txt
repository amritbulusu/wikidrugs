'''Flephedrone''', also known as '''4-fluoromethcathinone''' ('''4-FMC'''), is a [[stimulant]] [[drug]] of the [[substituted cathinone|cathinone]] [[chemical class]] that has been sold online as a [[designer drug]] starting in 2008.<ref>{{cite journal | url=http://www.sciencedirect.com/science/article/pii/S0379073808004659 | title=Fluoromethcathinone, a new substance of abuse | author1=R.P. Archer | journal=Forensic Science International | date=March 2009 | volume=185 | issue=1–3 | pages=10–20 | doi=10.1016/j.forsciint.2008.11.013 | pmid=19195800}}</ref>

== Toxicity ==
Flephedrone has only a short history of human use and its toxicity is not well established.<ref>{{Cite journal | last1 = Thornton | first1 = S. L. | last2 = Gerona | first2 = R. R. | last3 = Tomaszewski | first3 = C. A. | doi = 10.1007/s13181-012-0232-4 | title = Psychosis from a Bath Salt Product Containing Flephedrone and MDPV with Serum, Urine, and Product Quantification | journal = Journal of Medical Toxicology | volume = 8 | issue = 3 | pages = 310–313 | year = 2012 | pmid =  22528592| pmc =3550171 }}</ref>

== Legality ==

Flephedrone has been illegal in Denmark since December 2008.{{Citation needed|date=March 2009}}<!-- can't find citation but the Danish government put out a press release in December 2008 reporting the banning of [[mephedrone]], flephedrone and one other RC so it should be easy enough to find for anyone who speaks Danish -->

Flephedrone has been illegal in the UK since April 2010.

Flephedrone has been illegal in the Lithuania since April 2010.

Flephedrone is most likely illegal in Australia as an analogue or derivative of cathinone.

Flephedrone has been illegal in Poland since April 2010.<ref>{{cite web|title=Ustawa z dnia 15 kwietnia 2011 r. o zmianie ustawy o przeciwdziałaniu narkomanii ( Dz.U. 2011 nr 105 poz. 614 )|url=http://isap.sejm.gov.pl/DetailsServlet?id=WDU20111050614|publisher=Internetowy System Aktów Prawnych|accessdate=17 June 2011}}</ref>

Flephedrone has been classed as a narcotic in Sweden since October 2010.<ref name="Ban on Flephedrone in sweden">[http://www.lakemedelsverket.se/upload/lvfs/LVFS_2010_23.pdf], Medical Products Agency (Sweden), Läkemedelsverkets författningssamling (Swedish)</ref>

In the United States, flephedrone is temporarily listed as a Schedule I controlled substance.<ref name=federalregister/>  It is permanently listed as a Schedule I controlled substance in Florida,<ref>http://www.myfloridalicense.com/dbpr/abt/documents/BathSaltsDrugEmergencyRule.pdf</ref> Georgia, and Pennsylvania.<ref>http://www.legis.state.pa.us/cfdocs/legis/PN/Public/btCheck.cfm?txtType=HTM&sessYr=2011&sessInd=0&billBody=S&billTyp=B&billNbr=1006&pn=1304</ref>

As of October 2015 4-FMC is a controlled substance in China.<ref>{{cite web | url=http://www.sfda.gov.cn/WS01/CL0056/130753.html | title=关于印发《非药用类麻醉药品和精神药品列管办法》的通知 | publisher=China Food and Drug Administration | date=27 September 2015 | language=Chinese | accessdate=1 October 2015}}</ref>
