'''Iotrolan''' (trade name '''Isovist''') is a [[radiocontrast agent]], a substance used to improve the visibility of body structures on images obtained by [[X-ray]] techniques. It is particularly used to image spaces surrounding the [[central nervous system]], such as the [[ventricular system|ventricle]]s, after injection into the [[cerebrospinal fluid]].<ref>[http://home.intekom.com/pharm/schering/isovist.html Package insert]</ref> It can also be used to image joint spaces and in [[endoscopic retrograde cholangiopancreatography]] (ERCP).{{fact|date=September 2011}}

Chemically, the substance is a [[Dimer (chemistry)|dimer]] of triiodinated [[isophthalic acid]] derivatives.
