'''Gitoformate''' ([[International Nonproprietary Name|INN]], or '''pentaformylgitoxin''', trade name '''Dynocard''') is a [[cardiac glycoside]], a type of drug that can be used in the treatment of [[congestive heart failure]] and [[cardiac arrhythmia]] (irregular heartbeat).<ref>{{Cite journal 
| last1 = Rietbrock | first1 = N. 
| last2 = Woodcock | first2 = B. G. 
| last3 = Hrazdil | first3 = U.

| journal = Arzneimittel-Forschung 
| volume = 34 
| issue = 8 
| pages = 915–917 
| year = 1984 
| pmid = 6541927
}}</ref> Produced by [[Madaus]], it is not available in the US, and does not seem to be available in Europe either.
