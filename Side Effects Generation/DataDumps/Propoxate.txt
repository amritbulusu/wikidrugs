'''Propoxate''' ([[International Nonproprietary Name|INN]]; '''R7464''') is an [[anesthetic]] related to [[etomidate]] and [[metomidate]] that was never marketed. Though it is not used in humans, it has been used as an anesthetic in [[fish]].<ref name="RossRoss2009">{{cite book|author1=Lindsay G.
