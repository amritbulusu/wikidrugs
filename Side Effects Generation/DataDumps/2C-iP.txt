'''2C-iP''' (also known as '''Jelena''') is a relatively potent and long acting [[psychedelic drug|psychedelic]] [[phenethylamine]] and compound from the [[2C (psychedelics)|2C]] family that was first synthesized by Dmitri Ger and has been sold online as a [[designer drug]].<ref>{{cite web | url=http://bitnest.ca/external.php?id=%250E%251C%2517%2526x%255EYB%2519%2505 | title=Triple Goddess of the Night | publisher=British Neuroscience Association Bulletin | date=1 March 2011 | accessdate=16 July 2015 | author=Anna Ger, Dmitri Ger}}</ref><ref>{{cite web | url=http://countyourculture.com/2010/10/04/the-alkylated-2cs/ | title=The alkylated 2Cs | publisher=countyourculture | date=4 October 2010 | accessdate=16 July 2015 | author=B. Meyers-Riggs}}</ref>

It is a [[structural analog]] of [[2C-P]].

==Legality==

===Canada===
As of October 31st, 2016; 2C-iP is a controlled substance (Schedule III) in Canada. http://gazette.gc.ca/rp-pr/p2/2016/2016-05-04/html/sor-dors72-eng.php
