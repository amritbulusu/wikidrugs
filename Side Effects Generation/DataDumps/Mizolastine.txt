'''Mizolastine''' ('''Mizollen''') is a once-daily, non-sedating [[Antihistamine#H1-antihistamines|antihistamine]]. It blocks [[H1 receptor|H<sub>1</sub> receptor]]s and is commonly fast-acting. It does not prevent the actual release of histamine from [[mast cell]]s, just prevents it binding to receptors. Side effects can include dry mouth and throat.<ref>{{Cite web
