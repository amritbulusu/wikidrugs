==Synthesis==
The [[amide]] [[proton]] from 4-Benzylphalazone is abstracted with KOH, which is then [[alkylated]] with diethylaminoethyl [[chloride]].<ref>Engelbrecht et al., {{Cite patent|DE|1046625}}; eidem, {{US patent|3017411}} (1958, 1962, both to VEB Deutsches Hydrierwerk Rodleben).</ref>
