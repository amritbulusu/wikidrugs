'''Fluorenone''' is an [[aromatic]] [[organic compound]] with the chemical formula C<sub>13</sub>H<sub>8</sub>O. It is used to make [[antimalaria drug]]s. It can be synthesised from [[fluorenol]] with the addition of [[glacial acetic acid]] and [[sodium hypochlorite]] solution, undergoing an oxidation reaction. It is bright fluorescent yellow in colour and is a solid at room temperature.
