'''Oxipurinol''' ([[International Nonproprietary Name|INN]], or '''oxypurinol''' [[United States Adopted Name|USAN]]) is an [[enzyme inhibitor|inhibitor]] of [[xanthine oxidase]]. It is an [[active metabolite]] of [[allopurinol]] and it is cleared renally. In cases of renal disease, this metabolite will accumulate to toxic levels. By inhibiting xanthine oxidase, it reduces [[uric acid]] production. High serum uric acid levels may result in [[gout]], [[kidney stones]], and other medical conditions.
