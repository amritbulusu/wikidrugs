==Synthesis==
[[File:Phenaglycodol synthesis.svg|thumb|center|700px|Jack Mills "2-chlorophenyl-3-methyl-2, 3-butanediols" {{US patent|2812363}} (1957 to [[Eli Lilly and Company|Eli Lilly Co.]]).]]
''p''-Chloroacetophenone and [[sodium cyanide|NaCN]] are reacted together to give the corresponding [[cyanohydrin]] (cf [[Strecker synthesis]]). The cyano group is then hydrated in acid to the corresponding amide, thus ''p''-chloro[[atrolactamide]] ('''4''') is formed. The amide group is then further hydrolyzed with a 2nd equivalent of water in concentrated lye to ''p''-chloro[[atrolactic acid]] ('''5'''); this is then esterified to Ethyl ''p''-chloroatrolactate ('''6'''). Finally, [[nucleophilic addition]] a couple of equivalents of MeMgI are added to the ester give Phenaglycodol ('''7''') crystals.
