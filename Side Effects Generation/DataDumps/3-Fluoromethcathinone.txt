'''3-Fluoromethcathinone'''  (also known as '''3-FMC''' is a [[stimulant]] of the [[substituted phenethylamine|phenethylamine]], [[substituted amphetamine|amphetamine]] and [[substituted cathinone|cathinone]] [[chemical class]]es that has been sold online as a [[designer drug]].<ref>{{cite journal | url=http://jat.oxfordjournals.org/content/early/2015/10/06/jat.bkv113.abstract | title=Analysis of Amphetamine-Derived Designer Drugs by Gas Chromatography with Mass Spectrometry | author=Peter Ondra | author2=Ivo Válka | author3=Radim Knob| author4=Pavlína Ginterová | author5=Vítězslav Maier | journal=Journal of Analytical Toxicology | date=October 2015 | doi=10.1093/jat/bkv113 | pmid=26446487 | pages=bkv113}}</ref><ref name=crew>{{cite journal|pmid=19195800|title= Fluoromethcathinone, a new substance of abuse. | doi=10.1016/j.forsciint.2008.11.013 | volume=185|issue=1-3|date=March 2009|journal=Forensic Sci. Int.|pages=10–20|author=Archer RP}}</ref> It is a [[structural isomer]] of [[flephedrone]] (4-fluoromethcathinone).

3-Fluoro''iso''methcathinone is produced as a by-product when 3-FMC is synthesized, the activity of this compound is unknown.<ref name="davies">[http://www.the-ltg.org/data/uploads/posters/methcase.pdf Two cases of confirmed ingestion of the novel designer compounds: 4-methylmethcathinone (Mephedrone) and 3-fluoromethcathinone] Susannah Davies ''et al.''</ref>

== Effects ==
This chemical's psychoactive effects are yet to be studied scientifically; however, it is suspected to be much like other methcathinones is likely a releaser or reuptake inhibitor of Catecholamines. It is also suspected to lack entactogenic quality.<ref name="davies" />

== Legal status ==
In the United States, 3-FMC is [[Controlled Substances Act|Schedule I controlled substance]].<ref name=federalregister>[https://www.federalregister.gov/articles/2014/03/07/2014-04997/schedules-of-controlled-substances-temporary-placement-of-10-synthetic-cathinones-into-schedule-i Schedules of Controlled Substances: Temporary Placement of 10 Synthetic Cathinones Into Schedule I]</ref>

In the United Kingdom, 3-FMC is a controlled drug under the cathinone [[Drugs controlled by the UK Misuse of Drugs Act|blanket ban]].

3-FMC is an [[Drugs controlled by the German Betäubungsmittelgesetz|Anlage II]] controlled drug in Germany.

3-FMC is banned in the Czech Republic.<ref>{{cite web | url=http://www.mzcr.cz/Admin/_upload/files/3/Nov%C3%A9%20PL.pdf | title=Látky, o které byl doplněn seznam č. 4 psychotropních látek (příloha č. 4 k nařízení  vlády č. 463/2013 Sb.) | publisher=Ministerstvo zdravotnictví | language=Czech}}</ref>
