'''Savage Laboratories''' is a [[pharmacology|pharmaceutical]] research company based in [[Melville, New York]]. Its two main products are [[CroFab]]&mdash;the primary [[antivenin]] used to treat [[rattlesnake]] and [[Agkistrodon piscivorus|cottonmouth]] envenomations in the United States&mdash;and [[DigiFab]]&mdash;a treatment for [[digoxin]] overdose, both of which are produced by [[BTG plc]] but distributed by Savage.{{citation needed|date=January 2014}} It also produces other products such as [[Ethiodol]] (a diagnostic agent for use in [[hysterosalpingography]] and [[lymphography]]), [[Evac-Q-Kwik]] (a bowel evacuant), KAON-CL (extended release [[Potassium chloride]]), and KAON Elixir-Grape ([[Potassium gluconate]]).

Savage Laboratories is a division of [[Altana|Altana AG]]'s American subsidiary, Altana Inc.
