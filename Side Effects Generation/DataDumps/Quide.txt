The '''Quide''' were a band of [[Indigenous peoples of the Americas|American Indians]], friends of the [[Jumano]], in the area around what is now [[Texas]].  In 1683, [[Juan Sabeata]] reported them as desiring [[missionary|missionaries]]. They are recorded as having lived three days' travel east of the mouth of the [[Concho River]].
