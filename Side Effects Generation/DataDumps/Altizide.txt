'''Altizide''' is a thiazide [[diuretic]]. Together with [[spironolactone]] it's sold under the brand name of '''Aldactacine''' and '''Aldactazine''' by [[Pfizer]] and other names by other companies.<ref>{{cite web|title=Altizide|url=http://www.drugs.com/international/altizide.html|website=drugs.com}}</ref>
