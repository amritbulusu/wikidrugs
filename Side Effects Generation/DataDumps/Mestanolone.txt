'''Mestanolone''' (brand names '''Andoron''', '''Notandron'''), also known as '''methylandrostanolone''', is an [[orally active]] [[anabolic-androgenic steroid]] (AAS) and the [[17α-alkylated anabolic steroid|17α-methylated]] [[chemical derivative|derivative]] of [[dihydrotestosterone]] (DHT).<ref name="Elks2014">{{cite book|author=J. Elks|title=The Dictionary of Drugs: Chemical Data: Chemical Data, Structures and Bibliographies|url=https://books.google.com/books?id=0vXTBwAAQBAJ&pg=PA775|date=14 November 2014|publisher=Springer|isbn=978-1-4757-2085-3|pages=775–}}</ref><ref name="Llewellyn2009">{{cite book|author=William Llewellyn|title=Anabolics|url=https://books.google.com/books?id=afKLA-6wW0oC|year=2009|publisher=Molecular Nutrition Llc|isbn=978-0967930473|pages=241}}</ref>

==Side effects==
{{See also|Anabolic steroid#Adverse effects}}

==Pharmacology==
Due to inactivation by [[3α-hydroxysteroid dehydrogenase]] (3α-HSD) in [[skeletal muscle]], mestanolone is described as a very poor [[anabolic]] agent, similarly to DHT.<ref name="Llewellyn2009" /> As mestanolone is already [[5α-reductase|5α-reduced]], it cannot be [[aromatase|aromatized]] and hence has no propensity for [[estrogen]]ic [[side effect]]s such as [[gynecomastia]].<ref name="Llewellyn2009" /> The drug also has no [[progestogen]]ic activity.<ref name="Llewellyn2009" /> Like other 17α-alkylated AAS, mestanolone is [[hepatotoxic]].<ref name="Llewellyn2009" />

==Chemistry==
{{See also|List of androgens/anabolic steroids}}
Mestanolone, also known as '''17α-methyl-5α-dihydrotestosterone''' ('''17α-methyl-DHT''') or as '''17α-methyl-5α-androstan-17β-ol-3-one''', is a [[synthetic compound|synthetic]], [[17α-alkylated AAS|17α-alkylated]] [[androstane]] [[steroid]] and a [[chemical derivative|derivative]] of DHT differing from it only in the presence of a [[methyl group]] at the C17α position.<ref name="Elks2014" /><ref name="Llewellyn2009" /> Close synthetic relatives of mestanolone include [[oxandrolone]] (2-oxa-17α-methyl-DHT), [[oxymetholone]] (2-hydroxymethylene-17α-methyl-DHT), and [[stanozolol]] (a derivative of 17α-methyl-DHT (mestanolone) with a [[pyrazole]] [[ring (chemistry)|ring]] fused to the A ring).<ref name="Elks2014" /><ref name="Llewellyn2009" />
