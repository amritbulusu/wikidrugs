'''Mofebutazone''' (or '''monophenylbutazone''') is a drug used for joint and muscular [[pain]]. It is a 3,5-pyrazolinedione derivative.

The drug binds to plasma albumin and competes with drugs such as [[coumarin]] anticoagulants, [[indomethacin]] and [[glucocorticoid]]s.
