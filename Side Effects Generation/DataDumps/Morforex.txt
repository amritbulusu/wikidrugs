'''Morforex''' ([[International Nonproprietary Name|INN]]; '''Bo 637'''), also referable to as '''''N''-morpholinoethylamphetamine''', is an [[anorectic]] which was never marketed.<ref name="GanellinTriggle1996">{{cite book|author1=C.R. Ganellin|author2=David J. Triggle|title=Dictionary of Pharmacological Agents|url=https://books.google.com/books?id=A0THacd46ZsC&pg=PA1377|date=21 November 1996|publisher=CRC Press|isbn=978-0-412-46630-4|pages=1377–}}</ref><ref name="Organization2000">{{cite book|author=World Health Organization|title=International Nonproprietary Names (INN) for Pharmaceutical Substances|url=https://books.google.com/books?id=HcogPwAACAAJ|year=2000|publisher=World Health Organization|isbn=978-0-11-986227-0}}</ref>
