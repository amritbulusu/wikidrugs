'''Thiopropazate''' ('''Artalan''', '''Dartal''', '''Dartalan''', '''Dartan''') is a [[typical antipsychotic]] of the [[phenothiazine]] class.<ref name="isbn0-412-54090-8">{{cite book | author = | title = Dictionary of organic compounds | publisher = Chapman & Hall | location = London | year = 1996 | page = 5148 | isbn = 0-412-54090-8 | oclc = | doi = | url = https://books.google.com/books?id=r7z8W69GcoMC&lpg=PA5148&dq=thiopropazate%20neuroleptic&as_brr=3&pg=PA5148#v=onepage&q=&f=false}}</ref> It is a prodrug to [[perphenazine]].

Thiopropazate is manufactured by Searle (US, UK)  & Boehringer Mannheim (Germany)<ref>Pharmaceutical Manufacturing Encyclopedia p. 3209</ref>
Thiopropazate is sold by Chembase, AAA Chemistry, ZINC, AKos Consulting & Solutions, Boc Sciences, ChemFrog, and ChemMol<ref>https://pubchem.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/compound/thiopropazate#section=Top</ref>
