Elks|title=The Dictionary of Drugs: Chemical Data: Chemical Data, Structures and Bibliographies|url=https://books.google.com/books?id=0vXTBwAAQBAJ&pg=PA292|date=14 November 2014|publisher=Springer|isbn=978-1-4757-2085-3|pages=292–}}</ref><ref name="IndexNominum2000">{{cite book|title=Index Nominum 2000: International Drug Directory|url=https://books.google.com/books?id=5GpcTQD_L2oC&pg=PA252|year=2000|publisher=Taylor & Francis|isbn=978-3-88763-075-1|pages=252–}}</ref> A [[propionate]] [[ester]] of clobetasol, [[clobetasol propionate]], has also been marketed, and is far more widely used in comparison.<ref name="Elks2014" /><ref name="IndexNominum2000" />
