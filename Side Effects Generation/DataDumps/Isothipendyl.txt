'''Isothipendyl''' is a 1st generation [[H1 antagonist|H<sub>1</sub> antagonist]] ([[antihistamine]]) and [[anticholinergic]] used as an [[antipruritic]]. It is nowadays{{when|date=September 2017}} scarcely used in the 1st line relief of allergies due to the anticholinergic side effect of [[somnolence]] but does have some limited use through [[topical application]] in the relief of [[insect bites]] and related itching ([[pruritus]]).
