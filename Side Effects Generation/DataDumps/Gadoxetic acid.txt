'''Gadoxetic acid''' is a [[gadolinium-based MRI contrast agent]]. Its salt, '''gadoxetate disodium''', is marketed as '''Primovist''' in Europe and '''Eovist''' in the United States by [[Bayer HealthCare Pharmaceuticals]].<ref name="bayer">{{cite web |url=http://imaging.bayerhealthcare.com/html/eovist/index.html |title=Eovist - Homepage |work= |accessdate=2009-03-22}}</ref>
