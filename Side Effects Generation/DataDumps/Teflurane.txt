Klein|title=A glossary of anesthesia and related terminology|url=https://books.google.com/books?id=gF9qAAAAMAAJ|year=1993|publisher=Springer-Verlag|isbn=978-0-387-97831-4}}</ref><ref name="ArtusioMazzia1962">{{cite book|author1=Joseph Francis Artusio|author2=Valentino D.
