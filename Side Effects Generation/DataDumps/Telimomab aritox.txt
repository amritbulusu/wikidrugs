'''Telimomab aritox''' is a [[mouse]] [[monoclonal antibody]] which is an [[immunosuppressive drug]]. The antibody is linked to the A chain of the [[ricin]] protein (which is reflected by the ''aritox'' in the drug's name).<ref>[http://apps.who.int/medicinedocs/index/assoc/h5791e/h5791e.pdf WHO Drug Information]</ref>
