'''Clocinizine''' is a [[first-generation antihistamine]] of the [[diphenylmethylpiperazine]] class. It is marketed in [[Spain]] in [[combination drug|combination]] with [[phenylpropanolamine]] under the brand name '''Senioral'''.<ref name="Drugs.com">https://www.drugs.com/international/clocinizine.html</ref>
