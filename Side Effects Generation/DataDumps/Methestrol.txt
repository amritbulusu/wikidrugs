'''Methestrol''' ([[International Nonproprietary Name|INN]]) or '''methoestrol''', also known as '''promethestrol''' or '''promethoestrol''' ([[British Approved Name|BAN]]) or as '''dimethylhexestrol''', is a [[Organic compound#Synthetic compounds|synthetic]], [[nonsteroidal]] [[estrogen]] of the [[stilbestrol]] group related to [[diethylstilbestrol]] that was never marketed.<ref name="GanellinTriggle1996">{{cite book|author1=C.R. Ganellin|author2=David J. Triggle|title=Dictionary of Pharmacological Agents|url=https://books.google.com/books?id=Z_mfTTIApVEC&pg=PA608|date=21 November 1996|publisher=CRC Press|isbn=978-0-412-46630-4|pages=608–}}</ref><ref name="BruckerKing2015">{{cite book|author1=Mary C. Brucker|author2=Tekoa L. King|title=Pharmacology for Women’s Health|url=https://books.google.com/books?id=ofCeCgAAQBAJ&pg=PT640|date=8 September 2015|publisher=Jones & Bartlett Learning|isbn=978-1-284-10811-8|pages=640–}}</ref>
