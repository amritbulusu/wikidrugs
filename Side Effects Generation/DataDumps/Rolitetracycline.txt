'''Rolitetracycline''' is a [[tetracycline antibiotic]]. Tetracycline is ''N''-[[Mannich base]] [[prodrug]] that is prepared from [[tetracycline]] by condensation with [[pyrrolidine]] and [[formaldehyde]] to produce rolitetracycline.
