'''Quipazine''' is a [[piperazine]] [[drug]] used in [[scientific research]]. It is a moderately [[binding selectivity|selective]] [[serotonin receptor]] [[receptor (biochemistry)|receptor]] [[Receptor agonist|agonist]], binding to a range of different [[serotonin]] receptors, but particularly to the [[5-HT2A receptor|5-HT<sub>2A</sub>]]<ref>{{cite journal|pmid=7473132|year=1995|author1=Smith|first1=R.
