'''3C-E''' is a [[psychedelic drug|psychedelic]] of the [[substituted amphetamine|amphetamine]] class. It was first synthesized by [[Alexander Shulgin]]. In his book ''[[PiHKAL|PiHKAL (Phenethylamines i Have Known And Loved)]]'', Shulgin lists the dosage range as 30 to 60&nbsp;mg, consumed orally. The duration of action was stated to be 8–12 hours.<ref>{{CitePiHKAL}}</ref>

This compound is the three-carbon chain analogue of [[escaline]].
