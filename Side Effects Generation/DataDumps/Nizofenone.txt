'''Nizofenone''' ('''Ekonal''', '''Midafenone''') is a [[neuroprotective]] drug which protects neurons from death following cerebral anoxia (interruption of oxygen supply to the brain). It might thus be useful in the treatment of acute neurological conditions such as [[stroke]].<ref>Ohta T, Kikuchi H, Hashi K, Kudo Y. Nizofenone administration in the acute stage following subarachnoid hemorrhage. Results of a multi-center controlled double-blind clinical study. ''Journal of Neurosurgery''. 1986 Mar;64(3):420-6. {{PMID|3512795}}</ref><ref>Yasuda H, Izumi N, Nakanishi M, Maruyama Y. Brain protection against oxygen deficiency by nizofenone. ''Advances in Experimental Medicine and Biology''. 1988;222:403-10. {{PMID|3364265}}</ref><ref>Yasuda H, Nakajima A. Brain protection against ischemic injury by nizofenone. ''Cerebrovascular and Brain Metabolism Reviews''. 1993 Winter;5(4):264-76. {{PMID|8110594}}</ref><ref>Matsumoto Y, Aihara K, Kamata T, Goto N. Nizofenone, a neuroprotective drug, suppresses glutamate release and lactate accumulation. ''European Journal of Pharmacology''. 1994 Sep 1;262(1-2):157-61. {{PMID|7813567}}</ref>
