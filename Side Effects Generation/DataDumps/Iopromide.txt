'''Iopromide''' is a molecule used as a [[contrast medium]]. It is marketed under the name '''Ultravist''' which is produced by Bayer Healthcare. It is a low [[osmolar]], [[non-ionic]] contrast agent for [[intravascular]] use.<ref>[http://imaging.bayerhealthcare.com/html/ultravist/index.html The entry on Bayer Healthcare's website about Ultravist]</ref>

It is commonly used in radiographic studies such as [[intravenous urogram]]s, brain [[computer tomography]] (CT) and [[CT pulmonary angiogram]]s (CTPAs).
