'''Cyclazodone''' is a centrally acting [[stimulant]] drug developed by American Cyanamid Company in the 1960s.<ref>US Patent 3,321,470 - 5-Arylidene-2Amino-2-Oxazolin-4-Ones</ref> The drug is related to other drugs such as [[pemoline]] and [[4-methylaminorex]]. It is a banned stimulant under the [[World Anti-Doping Agency]] prohibited list.<ref>[http://www.kada-ad.or.kr/download/2008_Prohibited_List.pdf World Anti-Doping Agency 2008 Prohibited List]{{dead link|date=August 2017 |bot=InternetArchiveBot |fix-attempted=yes }}</ref>
