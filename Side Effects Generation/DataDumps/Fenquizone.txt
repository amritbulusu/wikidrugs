'''Fenquizone''' ([[International Nonproprietary Name|INN]]) is a [[diuretic]], part of the class of low-ceiling sulfonamide diuretics. Fenquizone is used primarily in the treatment of oedema and hypertension. <ref>[http://www.mims.com/Page.aspx?menuid=mng&name=fenquizone&h=fenquizone&CTRY=TH fenquizone - drug information from MIMS (Thailand)<!-- Bot generated title -->]</ref>
