'''Invagesic''' is a [[combination drug]] consisting of:<ref>[http://www.reference.md/files/IN/INVAGESIC.html reference.md > Drug information for 'INVAGESIC'] Sources: NLM Medical Subject Headings, NIH UMLS, Drugs@FDA, FDA AERS original data copyright United States Government. * [[caffeine]]
* [[salicylic acid]]
* [[orphenadrine]]

It is indicated for management of [[fatigue (medical)|fatigue]], [[orthostatic hypotension]] and for the short term treatment of apnea of prematurity in infants.<ref>[http://pharmacycode.com/Invagesic.html Pharmacy Codes > Invagesic] Retrieved April 2011</ref>
