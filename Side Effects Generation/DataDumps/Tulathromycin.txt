'''Tulathromycin''' is a [[macrolide antibiotic]] used to treat [[bovine respiratory disease]] (BRD) in cattle and [[swine respiratory disease]] in pigs. It is marketed by [[Pfizer|Pfizer Inc.]] under the tradename '''Draxxin'''.

It is supplied as a solution for injection containing 100 mg of the drug. <ref>[http://www.draxxin.com/display.asp?country=US&lang=EN&drug=DR&species=DA&sec=600 Draxxin] at draxxin.com</ref>
