'''Cafedrine''' ([[International Nonproprietary Name|INN]]), also known as '''norephedrinoethyltheophylline''', is a chemical linkage of [[norephedrine]] and [[theophylline]] and is a [[cardiac stimulant]] used to increase [[blood pressure]] in people with [[hypotension]].
