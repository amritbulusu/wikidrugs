'''Razobazam''' ([[International Nonproprietary Name|INN]]) is a drug which is a [[benzodiazepine]] derivative. Its mechanism of action appears to be quite different from that of most benzodiazepine drugs, and it produces [[nootropic]] effects in animal studies.<ref>Hock FJ, Scheich H. Functional activity in the brain of socially deprivated rats produced by an active avoidance test after razobazam (Hoe 175) treatment: a 2-deoxyglucose study. ''Behavioural and Neural Biology''. 1986 Nov;46(3):398-409. {{PMID|3814045}}</ref>
