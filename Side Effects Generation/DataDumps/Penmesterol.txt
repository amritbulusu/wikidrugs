Elks|title=The Dictionary of Drugs: Chemical Data: Chemical Data, Structures and Bibliographies|url=https://books.google.com/books?id=0vXTBwAAQBAJ&pg=PA942|date=14 November 2014|publisher=Springer|isbn=978-1-4757-2085-3|pages=942–}}</ref><ref name="Azziz2007">{{cite book|author=Ricardo Azziz|title=Androgen Excess Disorders in Women|url=https://books.google.com/books?id=Ch-BsGAOtucC&pg=PA122|date=8 November 2007|publisher=Springer Science & Business Media|isbn=978-1-59745-179-6|pages=122–}}</ref><ref name="Publishing2013">{{cite book|author=William Andrew Publishing|title=Pharmaceutical Manufacturing Encyclopedia, 3rd Edition|url=https://books.google.com/books?id=_J2ti4EkYpkC&pg=PA2935-IA233|date=22 October 2013|publisher=Elsevier|isbn=978-0-8155-1856-3|pages=2935–}}</ref><ref name="ListHörhammer2013">{{cite book|author1=P.
