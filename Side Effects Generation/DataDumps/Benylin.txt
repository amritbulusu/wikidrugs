[[File:Benylin logo.png|thumb|The Benylin logo.]] '''Benylin''' is a [[Brand|brand name]] owned by [[Johnson & Johnson]] for a range of cough, cold and flu medications. Some Benylin products also contain [[codeine]], which is used to treat pain, cough and [[diarrhea]]. 

The flagship cough syrup and cold care brand is marketed in several countries as '''Benylin DM''', for its active ingredient, [[dextromethorphan]]. However, the range of products available in both solid dosage and liquid forms have formulations with multiple ingredients including [[pseudoephedrine]], [[paracetamol|paracetamol (acetaminophen)]] and [[guaifenesin]].
