'''Pipoxizine''' ({{abbrlink|INN|International Nonproprietary Name}}) is a [[first-generation antihistamine]] as well as [[serotonin antagonist]] of the [[diphenylmethylpiperazine]] group related to [[hydroxyzine]].<ref name="GanellinTriggle1996">{{cite book|author1=C.R. Ganellin|author2=David J.
