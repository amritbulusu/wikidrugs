'''Vindesine''' is an [[mitotic inhibitor|anti-mitotic]] [[vinca alkaloid]] used in [[chemotherapy]]. It is used to treat many different types of cancer, including [[leukaemia]], [[lymphoma]], [[melanoma]], [[breast cancer]], and [[lung cancer]].
