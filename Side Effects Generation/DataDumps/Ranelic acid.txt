'''Ranelic acid''' is an [[organic acid]] capable of [[chelation|chelating]] metal [[cation]]s.<ref>Bivalent metal salts of 2-N,N-di(carboxymethyl)amino,3-cyano,4-carboxymethyl,5-carboxy-thiophene-acid, process for their preparation and pharmaceutical compositions containing them.  {{cite patent|EP|415850}}</ref>

It forms the ranelate ion, C<sub>12</sub>H<sub>6</sub>N<sub>2</sub>O<sub>8</sub>S<sup>4âˆ’</sup>. [[Strontium ranelate]], the [[strontium]] [[salt (chemistry)|salt]] of ranelic acid, is a drug used to treat [[osteoporosis]] and increase [[bone mineral density]] (BMD).
