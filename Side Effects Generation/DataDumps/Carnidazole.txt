'''Carnidazole''' (trade names '''Spartrix''', '''Pantrix, Gambamix''') is an [[antiprotozoal drug]] of the [[nitroimidazole]] class used in [[veterinary medicine]]. It is used to treat ''[[Trichomonas]]'' infection in pigeons.<ref name=Merck/>
