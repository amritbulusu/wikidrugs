'''Roflurane''' ([[International Nonproprietary Name|INN]], [[United States Adopted Name|USAN]], code name '''DA-893''') is a [[halocarbon]] [[drug]] which was investigated as an [[inhalational anesthetic]] but was never marketed.<ref>{{cite book|title=United States Adopted Names (USAN).|url=https://books.google.com/books?id=xvlsAAAAMAAJ|year=1968|publisher=United States Pharmacopeial Convention.}}</ref><ref>{{cite book|title=Dissertation Abstracts|url=https://books.google.com/books?id=I0scAQAAMAAJ|date=May 1964|publisher=University Microfilms.}}</ref><ref>{{cite book|title=Reports on the Progress of Applied Chemistry|url=https://books.google.com/books?id=Q2E6AAAAMAAJ|year=1963|publisher=Society of Chemical Industry}}</ref><ref name="Hudlický1976">{{cite book|author=Miloš Hudlický|title=Chemistry of organic fluorine compounds: a laboratory manual with comprehensive literature coverage|url=https://books.google.com/books?id=FOqFAAAAIAAJ|year=1976|publisher=Ellis Horwood Limited|isbn=978-0-470-41835-2}}</ref>
