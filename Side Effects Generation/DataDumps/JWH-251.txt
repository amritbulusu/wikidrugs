'''JWH-251''' ('''1-pentyl-3-(2-methylphenylacetyl)indole''') is a [[synthetic cannabinoid]] from the [[phenylacetylindole]] family, which acts as a [[cannabinoid agonist]] with about 5x selectivity for [[Cannabinoid receptor 1|CB<sub>1</sub>]] with a [[Dissociation constant|K<sub>i</sub>]] of 29nM and 146nM at [[Cannabinoid receptor 2|CB<sub>2</sub>]]. Similar to the related 2'-methoxy compound [[JWH-250]], the 2'-[[chloro]] compound [[JWH-203]], and the 2'-[[bromine|bromo]] compound [[JWH-249]], JWH-251 has a [[phenyl]][[acetyl]] group in place of the [[naphthoyl]] ring used in most [[aminoalkylindole]] cannabinoid compounds.
