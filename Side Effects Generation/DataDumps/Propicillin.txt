'''Propicillin''' is a [[penicillin]]. Properties are similar to [[benzylpenicillin]] particularly used in [[streptococcal infections]], not resistant to [[penicillinase]]. It is acid resistant and can be used orally as the [[potassium salt]].{{cn|date=June 2015}}
