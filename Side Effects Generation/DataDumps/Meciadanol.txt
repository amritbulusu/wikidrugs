'''Meciadanol''' is a synthetic [[O-methylated flavanol]]. It is the 3-O-methylation of [[catechin]].

It inhibits the [[histidine decarboxylase]] in rats.<ref>Gastric protection by meciadanol. A new synthetic flavonoid-inhibiting histidine decarboxylase. Stanislaw J. Konturek, Mary Ellen Kitler, Tomasz Brzozowski and Tadeusz Radecki, Digestive Diseases and Sciences, Volume 31, Number 8 / 1986, Pages 847-852, {{doi|10.1007/BF01296054}}</ref>
