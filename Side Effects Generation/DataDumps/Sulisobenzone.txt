Toxicol. Pharmacol. |volume=33 |issue=3 |pages=285â€“99 |date=June 2001 |pmid=11407932 |doi=10.1006/rtph.2001.1476 |url=}}</ref><ref>[http://www.skincancer.org/understanding-uva-and-uvb.html Skin cancer foundation: Understanding UVA and UVB]</ref>

Its [[sodium]] salt, '''sulisobenzone sodium''', is also referred to as '''[[benzophenone-5]]'''.
