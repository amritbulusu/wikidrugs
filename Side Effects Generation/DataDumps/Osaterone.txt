'''Osaterone''' (developmental code name '''TZP-5258'''), also known as '''17α-hydroxy-6-chloro-2-oxa-6-dehydroprogesterone''', as well as '''2-oxachloromadinone''', is a [[steroid]]al [[antiandrogen]] and [[progestin]] that was never marketed.<ref name="IndexNominum2011">{{cite book|title=Index Nominum: International Drug Directory|url=https://books.google.com/books?id=penvpwAACAAJ|year=2011|publisher=MedPharm|isbn=978-3-8047-5053-1}}</ref> The C17α [[acetate]] [[ester]] of osaterone, [[osaterone acetate]], in contrast, has been marketed.<ref name="IndexNominum2011" />
