'''Clofexamide''' (or '''amichlophene''') is an [[antidepressant]] that was a component of the [[pharmaceutical drug|drug]] combination [[clofezone]], the other being the [[nonsteroidal anti-inflammatory drug]] (NSAID) [[phenylbutazone]].
