'''AM-2232''' ('''1-(4-cyanobutyl)-3-(naphthalen-1-oyl)indole''') is a drug that acts as a potent but unselective [[agonist]] for the [[cannabinoid receptor]]s, with a [[Dissociation constant|''K''<sub>i</sub>]] of 0.28&nbsp;nM at [[Cannabinoid receptor 1|CB<sub>1</sub>]] and 1.48&nbsp;nM at [[Cannabinoid receptor 2 (macrophage)|CB<sub>2</sub>]].<ref name="US 7241799">{{Ref patent2 | country = US | number = 7241799 | status = granted | title = Cannabimimetic indole derivatives | pubdate = 2004-11-05 | gdate = 2007-07-10 | pridate= 2004-11-05 | inventor = Makriyannis A, Deng H | assign1= }}</ref>
