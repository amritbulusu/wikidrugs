'''Azepindole''' ('''McN-2453''') is a [[tricyclic]] [[chemical compound|compound]] with [[antidepressant]] and [[antihypertensive]] effects that was developed in the late 1960s but was never marketed.<ref name="urlDictionary of pharmacological agents - Google Books">{{cite book | url = https://books.google.com/books?id=A0THacd46ZsC&lpg=RA2-PA1921&dq=azepindole&as_brr=3&pg=RA2-PA1921#v=onepage&q=azepindole&f=false | title = Dictionary of pharmacological agents - Google Books}}</ref>
