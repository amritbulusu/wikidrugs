'''Tienopramine''' is a [[tricyclic antidepressant]] (TCA) which was never marketed.<ref name="isbn0-412-46630-9">{{cite book | author = David J. Triggle | title = Dictionary of Pharmacological Agents | publisher = Chapman & Hall/CRC | location = Boca Raton | year = 1996 | pages = | isbn = 0-412-46630-9 | oclc = | doi = | url = https://books.google.com/books?id=A0THacd46ZsC&lpg=PA1976&ots=IIkjlIt792&dq=tienopramine&pg=PA1976#v=onepage&q&f=false}}</ref> It is an [[structural analog|analogue]] of [[imipramine]] where one of the [[benzene]] [[functional group|ring]]s has been replaced with a [[thiophene]] ring.
