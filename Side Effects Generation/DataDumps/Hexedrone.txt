== Legal status ==

In the UK, Hexedrone is a Class B drug under The [[Misuse of Drugs Act 1971]] (Amendment) Order 2010 following the [[Advisory Council on the Misuse of Drugs|ACMD]]'s report on [[substituted cathinone]] derivatives,<ref>{{cite web | url=http://www.gov.uk/government/uploads/system/uploads/attachment_data/file/119173/acmd-cathinodes-report-2010.pdf/ | title=ACMD Consideration of The Cathinones | publisher=ACMD | date=31 March 2010}}</ref> making it illegal to sell, buy, or possess without a license.<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.homeoffice.gov.uk/about-us/corporate-publications-strategy/home-office-circulars/circulars-2010/010-2010/ |title=A change to the Misuse of Drugs Act 1971 : Control of mephedrone and other cathinone derivatives |publisher=Home Office |date=16 April 2010 |deadurl=bot: unknown |archiveurl=http://webarchive.nationalarchives.gov.uk/20130125102358/http://www.homeoffice.gov.uk/about-us/corporate-publications-strategy/home-office-circulars/circulars-2010/010-2010/ |archivedate=25 January 2013 |df= }}</ref><ref>{{cite web | url=http://www.legislation.gov.uk/uksi/2010/1207/article/2/made | title=The Misuse of Drugs Act 1971 (Amendment) Order 2010 | publisher=Home Office | date=12 April 2010}}</ref>
