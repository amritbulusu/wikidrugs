'''CRL-40,941''' (also known as '''fladrafinil ''' and '''fluorafinil''') is a [[eugeroic]] closely related to [[adrafinil]] and [[modafinil]]. It is the bis(''p''-fluoro) ring-substituted derivative of adrafinil. CRL-40,941 was found to produce [[serenic|antiaggressive]] effects in animals, which adrafinil does not produce.<ref>{{ cite patent | country = US | status = patent | number = 4489095 | inventor = Lafon, L. | title = Halogenobenzhydrylsulfinylacetohydroxamic Acids | gdate = 1984-12-18 | assign1 = Laboratoire L. Lafon }}</ref>
