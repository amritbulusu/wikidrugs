'''Dichloropane''' ('''(−)-2β-Carbomethoxy-3β-(3,4-dichlorophenyl)tropane''', '''RTI-111''', '''O-401''') is a [[stimulant]] of the [[phenyltropane]] class that acts as a [[serotonin–norepinephrine–dopamine reuptake inhibitor]] (SNDRI) with [[IC50|IC<sub>50</sub>]] values of 3.13, 0.79 and 18 nM, respectively.<ref>{{cite journal|first1=F. ==Trans -CO<sub>2</sub>Me group==
The thermodynamic isomer with a trans -CO<sub>2</sub>Me group is still active. This isomer was used by Neurosearch to make three different phenyltropanes which were tested in clinical trials.

*[[Tesofensine]]
*[[Brasofensine]]
*[[NS-2359]] (GSK-372,475)
