'''Ipronidazole''' is an [[antiprotozoal drug]] of the [[nitroimidazole]] class used in [[veterinary medicine]].  It is used for the treatment of [[histomoniasis]] in turkeys and for swine [[dysentery]].<ref>[http://www.inchem.org/documents/jecfa/jecmono/v25je04.htm Ipronidazole], [[World Health Organization|WHO]] Food Additives Series 25</ref>
