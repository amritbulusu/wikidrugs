'''4-Ethylmethcathinone''' ('''4-EMC''') is a recreational [[designer drug]] of the [[stimulant]] and [[entactogen]] class. It is a structural isomer of [[4-MEC]].

== Legal status ==
4-Ethylmethcathinone is a controlled substance in the US state of Vermont as of January 2016.<ref>{{cite web | url=http://healthvermont.gov/regs/documents/regulated_drugs_rule_20160101.pdf | title=Regulated Drugs Rule | publisher=Vermont Department of Health | accessdate=14 October 2015}}</ref> It is also covered by analogue provisions in many other jurisdictions.
