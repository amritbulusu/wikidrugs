'''Nogalamycin''' is an [[anthracycline]] antibiotic produced by soil [[bacteria]] ''[[Streptomyces nogalater]]''. It has antitumor properties but it is also highly cardiotoxic. Less cardiotoxic semisynthetic analog [[menogaril]] was developed in the 1970s. Currently nogalamycin or menogaril aren't used clinically.<ref>[http://www.chemicalbook.com/ChemicalProductProperty_EN_CB3752063.htm NOGALAMYCIN]. ChemicalBook.com. Accessed November 28, 2012.</ref>
