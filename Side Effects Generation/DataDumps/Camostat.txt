'''Camostat''' ([[International Nonproprietary Name|INN]]) or '''FOY-305''' is a [[serine protease]] inhibitor. Serine protease enzymes have a variety of functions in the body, and so camostat has a diverse range of uses.  It is used in the treatment of some forms of [[cancer]] and is also effective against some [[virus|viral]] infections, as well as inhibiting [[fibrosis]] in liver or kidney disease or [[pancreatitis]].<ref>Okuno M, Kojima S, Akita K, Matsushima-Nishiwaki R, Adachi S, Sano T, Takano Y, Takai K, Obora A, Yasuda I, Shiratori Y, Okano Y, Shimada J, Suzuki Y, Muto Y, Moriwaki Y.
