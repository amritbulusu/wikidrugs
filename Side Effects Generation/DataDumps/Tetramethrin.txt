'''Tetramethrin''' is a potent synthetic [[insecticide]] in the [[pyrethroid]] family.<ref>[http://www.alanwood.net/pesticides/tetramethrin.html Tetramethrin], alanwood.net</ref>  It is a white [[crystal]]line [[solid]] with a [[melting point]] of 65-80 Â°C.  The commercial product is a mixture of [[stereoisomer]]s.

It is commonly used as an insecticide, and affects the insect's nervous system.  It is found in many household insecticide products.<ref>[http://householdproducts.nlm.nih.gov/cgi-bin/household/brands?tbl=chem&id=637&query=tetramethrin&searchas=TblChemicals Tetramethrin], Household Products Database</ref>
