'''Cyprodenate''' ('''Actebral''') is a [[stimulant]] drug.<ref>{{vcite journal |author=Dormard Y, Levron JC, Le Fur JM |title=Pharmacokinetic study of maleate acid of 2-(N,N-dimethylaminoethanol-14C1)-cyclohexylpropionate (cyprodenate) and of N,N-dimethylaminoethanol-14C1 in animals |journal=Arzneimittelforschung |date=1975 Feb |volume=25 |issue=2 |pages=201â€“7 |pmid=1173033 }}</ref> It was used for counteracting the effects of [[benzodiazepine]] tranquillizer drugs before the development of newer [[antidotes]] such as [[flumazenil]].<ref>{{vcite journal |author=Bobkov Yu.G., Morozov I.S. |title=Correction of psychodepressive effects of benzodiazepine tranquilizers by administration of psychoenergizers |language=English |journal=Bulletin of Experimental Biology and Medicine |date=1982 Oct |volume=94 |issue=4 |pages=1366-1369 |doi=10.1007/BF00827201 }}</ref> It produces [[dimethylethanolamine]] as a [[metabolite]], and so might be expected to produce [[nootropic]] effects.
