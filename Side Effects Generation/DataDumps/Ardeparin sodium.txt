'''Ardeparin''' (trade name '''Normiflo''') is an [[anticoagulant]]. It was used for the prevention of [[deep vein thrombosis]], but was withdrawn from the US market in 2000.<ref>Drugs.com: [http://www.drugs.com/mtm/normiflo.html Normiflo]</ref>
