'''Alfadolone''' ([[International Nonproprietary Name|INN]]), or '''alphadolone''' is a [[neuroactive steroid]] and [[general anesthetic]].<ref name="GanellinTriggle1996">{{cite book|author1=C.R. Ganellin|author2=David J. Triggle|title=Dictionary of Pharmacological Agents|url=https://books.google.com/books?id=A0THacd46ZsC&pg=PA1094|date=21 November 1996|publisher=CRC Press|isbn=978-0-412-46630-4|pages=1094–}}</ref> Along with [[alfaxolone]], as '''alfadolone acetate''', it is one of the components of the anesthetic [[combination drug|drug mixture]] [[Alfaxolone/alfadolone|althesin]].<ref name="GanellinTriggle1996" />
