[[File:Alpha-Pyrrolidinopentiophenone.svg|thumb|right|Chemical structure of [[alpha-Pyrrolidinopentiophenone|''alpha''-pyrrolidinopentiophenone]]]]'''Pyrrolidinophenones''' are a class of [[recreational drug|recreational]] [[designer drug]]s. The prototypical example is [[alpha-Pyrrolidinopentiophenone|''alpha''-pyrrolidinopentiophenone]] (α-PVP).

Pyrrolidinophenones have psychostimulant effects and are associated with [[central nervous system]] and [[cardiovascular]] toxicities.<ref>{{Cite journal | journal = Forensic Toxicology | date = 2014 | volume = 32 | issue = 1 | pages = 1–8 | title = Recently abused synthetic cathinones, α-pyrrolidinophenone derivatives: a review of their pharmacology, acute toxicity, and metabolism |author1=Kei Zaitsu |author2=Munehiro Katagi |author3=Hitoshi Tsuchihashi |author4=Akira Ishii | doi = 10.1007/s11419-013-0218-1}}</ref>
