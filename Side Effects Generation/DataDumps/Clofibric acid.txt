'''Clofibric acid''' is a [[herbicide]] with the [[IUPAC]] name 2-(4-chlorophenoxy)-2-methylpropanoic acid and [[molecular formula]] C<sub>10</sub>H<sub>11</sub>ClO<sub>3</sub>. It functions as a [[plant growth]] regulator against the [[plant hormone]] [[auxin]].<ref>[http://www.alanwood.net/pesticides/clofibric%20acid.html Alanwood.net: Clofibric acid]</ref>

Clofibric acid is a metabolite of the cholesterol-lowering pharmaceutical drug [[clofibrate]].

Clofibric acid [[derivative (chemistry)|derivatives]] include [[etofibrate]], [[pirifibrate]], and [[picafibrate]].
