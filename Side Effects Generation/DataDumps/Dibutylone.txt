'''Dibutylone''' (bk-DMBDB,<ref>[http://forendex.southernforensic.org/index.php/detail/index/1168 Southern Association of Forensic Scientists]</ref>) is a [[stimulant drug]] of the [[amphetamine]], [[phenethylamine]], and [[cathinone]] drug classes. It is structurally related to [[butylone]], a [[designer drug]] that has been detected in products marketed as [[Bath salts (drug)|bath salts]] or plant food.
