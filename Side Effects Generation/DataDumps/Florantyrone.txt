'''Florantyrone''' ([[International Nonproprietary Name|INN]]; also known as '''Fluorantyrone''') is a drug used in the treatment of [[biliary dyskinesia]]. It is also known as a [[cholagogue]] and [[choleretic]].<ref name=ncbi>{{cite web|title=florantyrone - Compound Summary|url=https://pubchem.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/summary/summary.cgi?q=all&cid=10617|publisher=PubChem Compound - [[National Center for Biotechnology Information]]|accessdate=15 November 2011}}</ref> 

It is manufactured from [[fluoranthene]] and [[succinic anhydride]] in the presence of [[aluminum chloride]] in a [[nitrobenzene]] solution. (US PATENT 2,560,425 (1951 To Miles Lab)).
