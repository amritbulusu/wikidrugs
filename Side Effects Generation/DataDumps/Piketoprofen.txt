'''Piketoprofen''' ([[International Nonproprietary Name|INN]]; trade names '''Calmatel''', '''Picalm''') is a [[nonsteroidal anti-inflammatory drug]] (NSAID) for topical use in form of a cream.<ref>{{cite journal|pmid=3487466|year=1986|author1=Fabregas|first1=J. L.|title=Percutaneous absorption of piketoprofen in rabbits: Effect of nonionic surface-active agents|journal=Il Farmaco; edizione pratica|volume=41|issue=5|pages=177–83|last2=Cucala|first2=J|last3=Segura|first3=J|last4=Tarrus|first4=E}}</ref><ref>{{cite journal|title=Double-Blind, Double-Dummy Comparative Study of Local Action Transcutaneous Flurbiprofen (Flurbiprofen LAT) versus Piketoprofen Cream in the Treatment of Extra-Articular Rheumatism|journal=Clin Drug Invest|year=2001|volume=21|issue=2|pages=95–102|doi=10.2165/00044011-200121020-00002|last1=Burgos|first1=A.|last2=Pérez Busquier|first2=M.|last3=Gómez Reino|first3=J.|last4=Ferreiro|first4=J.L.|last5=Navarro|first5=F.|last6=Valverde|first6=J.|last7=Moreno|first7=E.}}</ref>

Chemically, it is the 4-[[picoline]]amide of the NSAID [[ketoprofen]].
