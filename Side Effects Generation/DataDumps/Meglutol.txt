'''Meglutol''' ([[International Nonproprietary Name|INN]], also known as '''3-hydroxy-3-methylglutaric acid''', '''β-hydroxy-β-methylglutaric acid''', and '''dicrotalic acid''') is a [[hypolipidemic agent]].

It occurs free in ''[[Crotalaria dura]]'' and ''[[Crotalaria globifera|C. globifera]]'' and is bound in [[dicrotaline]] ([[pyrrolizidine alkaloid]]).
