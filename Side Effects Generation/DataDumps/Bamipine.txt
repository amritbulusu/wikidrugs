'''Bamipine''' (trade name '''Soventol''') is a pharmaceutical drug acting as an [[H1 antihistamine|H<sub>1</sub> antihistamine]] with [[anticholinergic]] properties. It is used as an [[antipruritic]] ointment.<ref>{{Cite patent |title = Pharmaceutical compositions containing tiotropium salts and antihistamines and their use |inventor = M Pairet, MP Pieper, CJM Meade |pubdate = 2001 |url = http://www.google.ca/patents?hl=en&lr=&vid=USPATAPP10007182&id=b0eWAAAAEBAJ&oi=fnd&dq=Bamipine+antihistamine+anticholinergic&printsec=abstract#v=onepage&q=Bamipine%20antihistamine%20anticholinergic&f=false}}</ref> No oral use is known.<ref>{{drugs.com|international|bamipine}}: Bamipine.</ref>

==Adverse effects==
SIde effects are typical of an old (first-generation) antihistamine: tiredness in adults, agitation in children, [[mydriasis]] (dilation of the pupils). These effects are rare when bamipine is applied topically as an ointment. Allergic and [[hypersensitivity]] reactions are also rare. Acute [[eczema]] can be worsened by bamipine ointment.<ref name="AC">{{cite book|title=Austria-Codex|at=Soventol-Gel|editor=Haberfeld, H|publisher=Ã–sterreichischer Apothekerverlag|location=Vienna|year=2015|language=German}}</ref>

==Contraindications and interactions==
No clinically relevant contraindications or interactions with other drugs are known.<ref name="AC" />

==Pharmacology==
===Pharmacokinetics===
When applied topically, the maximal effect is reached after 20 to 60 minutes and lasts up to 48 hours. Bamipine is not absorbed through intact skin in relevant doses.<ref name="AC" />
