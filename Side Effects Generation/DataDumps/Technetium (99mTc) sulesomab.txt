'''Technetium (<sup>99m</sup>Tc) sulesomab''' (trade name '''LeukoScan''') is a [[mouse]] [[monoclonal antibody]]<ref>[http://whqlibdoc.who.int/inn/proposed_lists/prop_INN_list75.pdf WHO Drug Information]</ref> labelled with [[technetium-99m]], a [[radionuclide]], for imaging with a [[gamma camera]].
