'''Sulfafurazole''' ([[International Nonproprietary Name|INN]], also known as '''sulfisoxazole''') is a [[sulfonamide (medicine)|sulfonamide]] [[antibacterial]] with an [[oxazole]] substituent. It has antibiotic activity against a wide range of [[Gram-negative bacteria|Gram-negative]] and [[Gram-positive bacteria|Gram-positive]] organisms.  It is sometimes given in combination with [[erythromycin]] (see [[erythromycin/Sulfafurazole]]) or [[phenazopyridine]]. It is used locally in a 4% solution or ointment.
