'''Cyclandelate''' is a [[vasodilator]] used in the treatment of [[claudication]], [[arteriosclerosis]] and [[Raynaud's disease]].  It is also used to treat [[Cramp#Nocturnal leg cramps|nighttime leg cramps]], and has been investigated for its effect against [[migraine]].  It is orally administered.
