'''Kitasamycin''' ([[International Nonproprietary Name|INN]]) is a [[macrolide antibiotic]]. It is produced by ''Streptomyces kitasatoensis''.{{Citation needed|date=May 2009}} The drug has antimicrobial activity against a wide spectrum of pathogens. There are several generic names of this drug such as-
•Kitasamycin (OS: BAN, JAN, USAN)
•Kitasamycine (OS: DCF)
•C 637 (IS)
•Katasamycin (IS)
•Leucomycin (IS)
•Kitasamycin (PH: JP XV)
•Kitasamycin Acetate (OS: JAN)
•Leucomycin Acetate (IS)
•Kitasamycin Acetate (PH: JP XV)
•Kitasamycin Tartrate (OS: JAN)
•Leucomycin Tartrate (IS)
•Kitasamycin Tartrate (PH: JP XV)
 ("Kitasamycin." - Drugs.com. N.p., n.d. Web. 5 July 2013.)
