Acylation of [[glycidol]] ('''2''') with the [[acid chloride]] '''1''' produces the ester '''3'''. Reaction of that intermediate with amine '''4''', obtained by reaction of 1,1-dimethylethylenediamine with urea, gives flestolol ('''5''').
