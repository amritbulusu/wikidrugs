'''Droxinavir''' (also known as '''SC 55389A''') was an experimental [[Protease inhibitor (pharmacology)|protease inhibitor]] researched by [[Pharmacia]] as a treatment for [[HIV/AIDS|HIV infection]]. Its research and development was discontinued on March 06, 1995.<ref>{{cite web|title=Drug Profile: Droxinavir|url=http://adisinsight.springer.com/drugs/800004768|website=AdisInsight|publisher=Adis International Ltd, part of Springer Science+Business Media|accessdate=23 November 2015}}</ref>
