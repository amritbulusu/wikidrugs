'''Dehydrocholic acid''' is a synthetic [[bile acid]], manufactured by the [[Redox|oxidation]] of [[cholic acid]]. It acts as a [[hydrocholeretic]], increasing bile output to clear increased bile acid load.<ref name="pmid3570165">{{cite journal |vauthors=Yousef IM, Barnwell SG, Tuchweber B, Weber A, Roy CC |title=Effect of complete sulfation of bile acids on bile formation in rats |journal=Hepatology |volume=7 |issue=3 |pages=535â€“42 |year=1987 |pmid=3570165 |doi=10.1002/hep.1840070320}}</ref>
