'''Medronic acid''' ([[conjugate acid|conjugate base]], '''medronate'''), also known as '''methylene diphosphonate''', is the smallest [[bisphosphonate]]. Its [[complex (chemistry)|complex]] with radioactive [[technetium]], [[technetium (99mTc) medronic acid|<sup>99m</sup>Tc medronic acid]], is used in [[nuclear medicine]] to detect bone abnormalities, including [[Metastasis|metastases]].
