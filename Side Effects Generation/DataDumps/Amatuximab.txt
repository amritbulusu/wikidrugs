'''Amatuximab''' is a chimeric [[monoclonal antibody]] designed for the treatment of cancer.<ref>{{cite web|title=Statement On A Nonproprietary Name Adopted By The USAN Council: Amatuximab|publisher=[[American Medical Association]]|url=http://www.ama-assn.org/resources/doc/usan/amatuximab.pdf}}</ref>

Amatuximab was developed by Morphotek, Inc.

Amatuximab is also known as '''Anti-mesothelin monoclonal antibody MORAb-009'''. Amatuximab is an anti-[[mesothelin]] monoclonal [[antibody]] [[MORAb-009]] binds to mesothelin (a [[protein]] that is made by some cancer cells) and stops the cells from dividing, and also called MORAb-009. {{uncited|date=October 2013}}
