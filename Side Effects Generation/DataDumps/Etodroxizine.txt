'''Etodroxizine''' ([[International Nonproprietary Name|INN]]) (brand names '''Vesparax''', '''Drimyl''', '''Indunox''', '''Isonox''') is a [[first-generation antihistamine]] of the [[diphenylmethylpiperazine]] group which is used as a [[sedative]]/[[hypnotic]] [[drug]] in [[Europe]] and [[South Africa]].<ref>{{cite book|title=Index Nominum 2000: International Drug Directory|url=https://books.google.com/books?id=5GpcTQD_L2oC&pg=PA418|date=January 2000|publisher=Taylor & Francis|isbn=978-3-88763-075-1|pages=418–}}</ref><ref name="Publishing2013">{{cite book|author=William Andrew Publishing|title=Pharmaceutical Manufacturing Encyclopedia, 3rd Edition|url=https://books.google.com/books?id=_J2ti4EkYpkC&pg=PA1525|date=22 October 2013|publisher=Elsevier|isbn=978-0-8155-1856-3|pages=1525–}}</ref><ref name="pmid2905131">{{cite journal|vauthors=Wolffgramm J, Lechner J, Coper H | title=Interaction of two barbiturates and an antihistamine on body temperature and motor performance of mice. | journal=Arzneimittelforschung | year= 1988 | volume= 38 | issue= 7 | pages= 885–91 | pmid=2905131 | doi= | pmc= | url=https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/entrez/eutils/elink.fcgi?dbfrom=pubmed&tool=sumsearch.org/cite&retmode=ref&cmd=prlinks&id=2905131}}</ref><ref name="pmid2905706">{{cite journal|vauthors=Maurer H, Pfleger K | title=Identification and differentiation of alkylamine antihistamines and their metabolites in urine by computerized gas chromatography-mass spectrometry. | journal=J Chromatogr | year= 1988 | volume= 430 | issue= 1 | pages= 31–41 | pmid=2905706 | doi= 10.1016/s0378-4347(00)83131-x| pmc= | url=https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/entrez/eutils/elink.fcgi?dbfrom=pubmed&tool=sumsearch.org/cite&retmode=ref&cmd=prlinks&id=2905706}}</ref>
