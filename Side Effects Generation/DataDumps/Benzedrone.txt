'''Benzedrone''' ('''4-MBC''') is a [[designer drug]] which has been found since 2010 as an ingredient in a number of "[[Bath salts (drug)|bath salt]]" mixes sold as recreational drugs.<ref>[http://www.emcdda.europa.eu/attachements.cfm/att_132857_EN_EMCDDA-Europol%20Annual%20Report%202010A.pdf EMCDDA Annual Report 2010]</ref><ref>[http://www.drugs.ie/features/feature/research_on_head_shop_drugs_in_dublin_part_2 Research on Head Shop drugs in Dublin: Part 2]</ref><ref>[http://www.nacd.ie/publications/Head_Report2011_overview.pdf An overview of new psychoactive substances and the outlets supplying them] {{webarchive|url=https://web.archive.org/web/20111125050141/http://www.nacd.ie/publications/Head_Report2011_overview.pdf |date=2011-11-25 }}</ref>
