'''Otilonium bromide''' is an [[antimuscarinic]]. It is used to relieve spasmodic pain of the gut especially [[irritable bowel syndrome]]<ref>http://www.grupounipharm.com/sites/default/files/DISMOX%20Inserto%20CORREGIDO%202011.%20Ingl%C3%A9s.pdf</ref> and is sold under the trade name '''Spasmoctyl 40''', and as '''Doralin''' in Greece.
