'''Flucindole''' is an [[antipsychotic]] with a [[tricyclic]] [[chemical structure|structure]], that was never marketed.<ref name="pmid6152347">{{cite journal | author = Wood PL, McQuade PS | title = Ciclindole and flucindole: novel tetrahydrocarbazolamine neuroleptics | journal = Progress in Neuro-psychopharmacology & Biological Psychiatry | volume = 8 | issue = 4-6 | pages = 773–7 | year = 1984 | pmid = 6152347 | doi = 10.1016/0278-5846(84)90057-5| url = }}</ref> It is the 6,8-difluoro derivative of [[cyclindole]].<ref name="pmid7011760">{{cite journal |author=Edelson J, Benziger DP |title=Disposition of a series of tetrahydrocarbazoles |journal=Drug Metabolism Reviews |volume=11 |issue=2 |pages=263–89 |year=1980 |pmid=7011760 |doi=10.3109/03602538008994027 |url=}}</ref>
