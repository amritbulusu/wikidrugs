'''Acedoben''' (4-acetamidobenzoic acid) is a [[chemical compound]] with the [[molecular formula]] of C<sub>9</sub>H<sub>9</sub>NO<sub>3</sub>.  It is the [[acetyl]] derivative of [[4-Aminobenzoic acid|''para''-aminobenzoic acid]] (PABA).

Acedoben, as a [[salt (chemistry)|salt]] with [[dimepranol]], is a component of some pharmaceutical preparations including [[inosine pranobex]].
