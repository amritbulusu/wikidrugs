'''Tantum''' is a small settlement located in the southwest corner of the island [[Brava, Cape Verde|Brava]], [[Cape Verde]]. It is one of the southernmost settlements in Cape Verde. It is situated in the mountains near the south coast, 6 km southwest of the island capital [[Nova Sintra]].

It is being the southernmost place in all of Cape Verde after nearby [[Cacha√ßo]] by about 250 meters.

At a small point 2.5 km southeast of the settlement, it is the closest point in Cape Verde to Brazil and the New World mainland which is 2,504 km SSW at [[Touros]], Rio Grande do Norte and is about 1,604 km nearly south to the tiny islets of the [[Saint Peter and Saint Paul Archipelago]].
