'''Metogest''' ([[International Nonproprietary Name|INN]], [[United States Adopted Name|USAN]]) (developmental code name '''SC-14207'''), also known as '''16,16-dimethyl-19-nortestosterone''', is a [[steroid]]al [[antiandrogen]] that was patented in 1975 and investigated as a treatment for [[acne]] but was never marketed.<ref name="Elks2014">{{cite book|author=J.
