'''Oxabolone cipionate''' ([[International Nonproprietary Name|INN]], [[Japanese Accepted Name|JAN]]) (brand names '''Steranabol Depo''', '''Steranabol Ritardo'''; former developmental code name '''FI-5852'''), or '''oxabolone cypionate''', also known as '''4-hydroxy-19-nortestosterone 17β-cypionate''' or '''estr-4-en-4,17β-diol-3-one 17β-cypionate''', is [[synthetic compound|synthetic]], [[injection (medicine)|injected]] [[anabolic-androgenic steroid]] (AAS) and [[chemical derivative|derivative]] of [[19-nortestosterone]] (nandrolone) that has been marketed in [[Europe]].<ref name="Elks2014">{{cite book|author=J. Elks|title=The Dictionary of Drugs: Chemical Data: Chemical Data, Structures and Bibliographies|url=https://books.google.com/books?id=0vXTBwAAQBAJ&pg=RA1-PA538|date=14 November 2014|publisher=Springer|isbn=978-1-4757-2085-3|pages=1–}}</ref><ref name="IndexNominum2000">{{cite book|title=Index Nominum 2000: International Drug Directory|url=https://books.google.com/books?id=5GpcTQD_L2oC&pg=PA764|date=January 2000|publisher=Taylor & Francis|isbn=978-3-88763-075-1|pages=764–765}}</ref><ref name="Krüskemper2013">{{cite book|author=H.-L. Krüskemper|title=Anabolic Steroids|url=https://books.google.com/books?id=4xIlBQAAQBAJ&pg=PA203|date=22 October 2013|publisher=Elsevier|isbn=978-1-4832-6504-9|pages=203–}}</ref> It is the C17β [[cypionate]] [[ester]] and a [[prodrug]] of [[oxabolone]] (4-hydroxy-19-nortestosterone).<ref name="Elks2014" /><ref name="IndexNominum2000" />
