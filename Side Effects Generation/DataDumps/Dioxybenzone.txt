'''Dioxybenzone''' ('''benzophenone-8''') is an [[organic compound]] used in [[sunscreen]] to block UVB and short-wave UVA ([[ultraviolet]]) rays.<ref>[http://www.skincancer.org/understanding-uva-and-uvb.html The Skin Cancer Foundation: Understanding UVA and UVB]</ref> It is a derivative of [[benzophenone]]. It is a yellow powder with a melting point of 68 Â°C.  It is insoluble in water, but moderately soluble in [[ethanol]] and [[isopropanol]].
