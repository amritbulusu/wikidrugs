'''Troxerutin''' is a [[flavonol]], a type of flavonoid. It is more accurately a [[hydroxyethylrutoside]]. It can be isolated from ''[[Sophora japonica]]'', the Japanese pagoda tree<!-- <ref>[ ]</ref> -->.

It is used as a [[vasoprotective]].<ref>{{Cite journal
