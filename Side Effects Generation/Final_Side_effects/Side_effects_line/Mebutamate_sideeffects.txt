'''Mebutamate''' ('''Capla''', '''Dormate''') is an [[anxiolytic]] and [[sedative]] [[drug]] with [[antihypertensive]] effects of the carbamate class.<ref name="TaylorFrancis2000">{{cite book | title = Index Nominum 2000: International Drug Directory | url = https://books.google.com/books?id=5GpcTQD_L2oC&pg=PA634 | date = January 2000 | publisher = Taylor & Francis | isbn = 978-3-88763-075-1 | page = 634}}</ref><ref>{{ cite book | title = The Merck Index | edition = 14 | publisher = Merck Publishers | isbn = 978-0-911910-00-1 | at = 5813 }}</ref> It has effects comparable to those of [[barbiturate]]s such as [[secobarbital]], but is only around 1/3 the potency of secobarbital as a sedative.
