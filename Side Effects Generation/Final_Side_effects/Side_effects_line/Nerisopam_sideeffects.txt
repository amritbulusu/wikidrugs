'''Nerisopam''' ('''GYKI-52322''', '''EGIS-6775''') is a drug which is a 2,3-[[benzodiazepine]] derivative, related to [[tofisopam]]. It has potent [[anxiolytic]] and [[neuroleptic]] effects in animal studies.<ref>Horváth K, Andrási F, Berzsenyi P, Pátfalusi M, Patthy M, Szabó G, Sebestyén L, Bagdy E, Körösi J, Botka P.
