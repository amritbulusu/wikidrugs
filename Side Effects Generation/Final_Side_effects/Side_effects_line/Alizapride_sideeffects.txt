'''Alizapride''' ('''Litican''', '''Plitican''', '''Superan''', '''Vergentan''') is a [[dopamine antagonist]] with [[prokinetic]] and [[antiemetic]] effects used in the treatment of [[nausea]] and [[vomiting]], including [[postoperative nausea and vomiting]].
