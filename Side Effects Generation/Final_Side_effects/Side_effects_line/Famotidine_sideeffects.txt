== Side effects ==

 
Side effects associated with famotidine use include [[headache]], [[dizziness]], and [[constipation]] or [[diarrhea]].<ref>{{cite web | url = http://www.rxlist.com/cgi/generic/famot_ad.htm | title = Pepcid Side Effects & Drug Interactions | year = 2008 | accessdate=31 July 2008 | publisher = RxList.com }}</ref>
