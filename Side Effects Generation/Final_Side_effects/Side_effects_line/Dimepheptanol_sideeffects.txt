'''Dimepheptanol''' ([[International Nonproprietary Name|INN]]; '''Amidol''', '''Pangerin'''), also known as '''methadol''' or '''racemethadol''', is a [[Chemical synthesis|synthetic]] [[opioid]] [[analgesic]] related to [[methadone]]. It has similar effects to other opioids, including [[analgesia]], [[sedation]] and [[euphoria]], as well as [[side effect]]s like [[itching]], [[nausea]] and [[respiratory depression]].
