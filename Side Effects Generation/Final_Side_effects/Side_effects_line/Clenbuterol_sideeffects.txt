==Effects and dosage==

 
Clenbuterol is a [[Beta2-adrenergic agonist|Î²<sub>2</sub> agonist]] with some structural and pharmacological similarities to [[epinephrine]] and [[salbutamol]], but its effects are more potent and longer-lasting as a stimulant and [[thermogenic]] drug.{{cn|date=August 2017}} It causes an increase in [[Aerobic exercise|aerobic]] capacity, [[central nervous system]] stimulation, [[blood pressure]], and [[oxygen]] transportation. It increases the rate at which body fat is metabolized while increasing the body's [[basal metabolic rate]] (BMR).{{cn|date=August 2017}} It is commonly used for [[smooth muscle]]-relaxant properties as a bronchodilator and [[tocolytic]].
