'''3C-P''' is a [[psychedelic drug|psychedelic]] [[phenethylamine]]. It has structural and pharmacodynamic properties similar to the drugs [[mescaline]], [[proscaline]], and [[amphetamine]].  Little information exists on the human [[pharmacology]] of 3C-P, but a psychedelic dosage appears to be 20-40mg, and is accompanied by [[stimulant]] effects.
