'''''N''-Ethylhexedrone''' (also known as '''α-ethylaminocaprophenone''', '''''N''-ethylnorhexedrone''', '''hexen''' and '''NEH''') is a [[stimulant]] of the [[substituted cathinone|cathinone]] class.<ref>{{cite journal|first1=Shuntaro|last1=Matsuta|first2=Munehiro|last2=Katagi|first3=Hiroshi|last3=Nishioka|first4=Hiroe|last4=Kamata|title=Structural characterization of cathinone-type designer drugs by EI mass spectrometry|url=https://www.jstage.jst.go.jp/article/jafst/19/2/19_77/_article|journal=Japanese Journal of Forensic Science and Technology|date=2014|pages=77–89|volume=19|issue=2|doi=10.3408/jafst.19.77|first5=Keiko|last5=Sasaki|first6=Noriaki|last6=Shima|first7=Tooru|last7=Kamata|first8=Akihiro|last8=Miki|first9=Michiaki|last9=Tatsuno|first10=Kei|last10=Zaitsu|first11=Kento|last11=Tsuboi|first12=Hitoshi|last12=Tsuchihashi|first13=Koichi|last13=Suzuki|language=Japanese}}</ref> ''N''-Ethylhexedrone was first mentioned in a series of patents by [[Boehringer Ingelheim]] in the 1960s<ref>{{citation|first1=Koeppe|last1=Herbert|first2=Zeile|last2=Karl|first3=Ludwig|last3=Gerhard|title=Patent DE1545591 - Verfahren zur Herstellung von α-Aminoketonen mit heterocyclischer Aminogruppe|url=https://worldwide.espacenet.com/publicationDetails/biblio?FT=D&date=19690807&DB=&locale=en_EP&CC=DE&NR=1545591A1&KC=A1|date=28 May 1965|language=de}}</ref> which led to the development of the better-known drug [[methylenedioxypyrovalerone]] (MDPV).<ref>{{cite journal|first1=Renata|last1=Kolanos|first2=Ernesto|last2=Solis|first3=Farhana|last3=Sakloth|first4=Louis J. De|last4=Felice|title=“Deconstruction” of the Abused Synthetic Cathinone Methylenedioxypyrovalerone (MDPV) and an Examination of Effects at the Human Dopamine Transporter|journal=ACS Chemical Neuroscience|date=18 December 2013|pages=1524–1529|volume=4|issue=12|pmid=24116392|pmc=3867964|doi=10.1021/cn4001236|first5=Richard A.|last5=Glennon}}</ref> Since the mid-2010s, ''N''-ethylhexedrone has been sold online as a [[designer drug]].<ref>{{cite web | url=http://nsddb.eu/substance/615/ | title=2-(Ethylamino)-1-phenylhexan-1-one | work=New Synthetic Drugs Database}}</ref><ref>{{cite web | url=http://www.policija.si/apps/nfl_response_web/0_Analytical_Reports_final/N-ethylhexedrone-ID-1503-16-report_final.pdf | title=Analytical Report - N-Ethylhexedrone | work=European Project RESPONSE}}</ref>
