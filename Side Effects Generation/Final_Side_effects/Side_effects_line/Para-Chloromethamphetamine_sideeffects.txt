'''''para''-Chloromethamphetamine''' (also known as '''4-Chloromethamphetamine''' and '''4-CMA''') is a [[stimulant]] that is the N-methyl derivative and prodrug of the neurotoxic drug [[Para-Chloroamphetamine|''para''-Chloroamphetamine]].<ref>{{cite journal | url=http://www.sciencedirect.com/science/article/pii/001429999190659E | title=[<sup>3</sup>H]Monoamine releasing and uptake inhibition properties of 3,4-methylenedioxymethamphetaimne and p-chloroamphetamine analogues |vauthors=Johnson MP, Conarty PF, Nichols DE | journal=European Journal of Pharmacology |date=July 1991  | volume=200 | issue=1 | pages=9–16 | doi=10.1016/0014-2999(91)90659-E | pmid=1685125}}</ref><ref>{{cite journal | url=http://www.sciencedirect.com/science/article/pii/0028390875900994 | title=Comparison of 4-chloro-, 4-bromo- and 4-fluoroamphetamine in rats: Drug levels in brain and effects on brain serotonin metabolism |author1=R.W.
