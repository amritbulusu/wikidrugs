'''Cathine''', also known as '''d-norpseudoephedrine''' and '''(+)-norpseudoephedrine''', is a [[psychoactive drug]] of the [[phenethylamine]] and [[amphetamine]] [[chemical class]]es which acts as a [[stimulant]].
