'''Tyrothricin''' is a cyclic [[polypeptide]]-antibiotic mixture from [[Bacillus brevis]].<ref>[http://www.sigmaaldrich.com/catalog/search/ProductDetail/SIGMA/T5629]</ref> It is a locally effective antibiotic effective against [[gram-positive]] bacteria. It is sometimes combined with [[benzocaine]] 5&nbsp;mg to provide relief from [[sore throat]]s. In systemic intake it can lead to severe side effects, therefore, the use is limited to topical application.

 
==Side-effects==

 
Known hypersensitivity reactions are common, temporary loss of balance (see [[Vestibular system]]) and nephrotoxic effects, i.e. impairment of renal function. There are no long term studies about its effect on pregnancy and lactation.
