Cefoxitin is sold in three major IV doses, 1g, 2g, and 10g.<ref>{{Cite web|url=http://www.catalog.md/drugs/mefoxin.html|title=Mefoxin Drug Information, Indications & Other Medicaments|last=|first=|date=|website=www.catalog.md|archive-url=|archive-date=|dead-url=|access-date=2017-04-28}}</ref> It is usually given to adults every six to eight hours in 1g or 2g doses.<ref name=":42">{{Cite news|url=http://www.rxlist.com/mefoxin-side-effects-drug-center.htm|title=Common Side Effects of Mefoxin (Cefoxitin) Drug Center - RxList|last=Cunha|first=John|date=|work=RxList|access-date=2017-05-02|archive-url=|archive-date=|dead-url=|language=en}}</ref> Cefoxitin may interfere with tests detecting urine glucose and result in a false positive.<ref name=":0">{{Cite web|url=https://edudrugs.com/M/Mefoxin.html|title=Mefoxin tablet :: Generic, Side effects, Interchangeable drugs, etc..|last=|first=|date=|website=edudrugs.com|language=en|archive-url=|archive-date=|dead-url=|access-date=2017-04-28}}</ref> As with any antibiotic, it should not be given to patients who are allergic to it.<ref name=":0" />

 
It is also used to treat pelvic inflammatory disease, because it is a broad spectrum antibiotic. For outpatient treatment, oral antibiotics or those with less frequent dosing may be prescribed.<ref>{{Cite book|url=http://hdl.handle.net/2027/uiug.30112023431809|title=Sexually transmitted diseases :treatment guidelines.|last=|first=|publisher=U.S. Department of Health and Human Services|year=|isbn=|location=Atlanta, GA|pages=}}</ref> As an effective alternative to [[Penicillin|penicilin]] and [[spectinomycin]], and replacement for methicillin, cefoxitin is used to treat gonorrhea in both men and women with few side effects.<ref name=":2" />
