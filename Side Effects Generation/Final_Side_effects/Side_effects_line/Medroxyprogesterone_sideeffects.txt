'''Medroxyprogesterone''' (abbreviated as '''MP'''), sold under the brand name '''Controlestril''', is a [[progestin]] which is used in [[veterinary medicine]] in [[France]] to control [[estrus]] in animals.<ref name="Elks2014">{{cite book|author=J. Morton|author2=Judith M.
