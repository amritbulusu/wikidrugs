The [[ethanol fermentation|fermentation]] of sugar into ethanol is one of the earliest [[biotechnology|biotechnologies]] employed by humans. The intoxicating effects of ethanol consumption have been known since ancient times. Ethanol has been used by humans since prehistory as the intoxicating ingredient of [[alcoholic beverage]]s.
