== Side effects ==

 
Common [[adverse drug reaction|side effects]] may include a depletion of [[sodium]], [[folic acid]] and [[calcium]], nausea, vomiting, diarrhea, headache, dizziness, fatigue, and dry mouth. Serious side effects may include heart palpitations, tingling/numbness, fever, chills, sore throat, rash, and back pain. Triamterene can also cause [[kidney stones]] through direct crystallization or by seeding [[calcium oxalate]] stones. Triamterene is best avoided in patients with chronic kidney disease due to the possibility of [[hyperkalemia]].
