'''Felbinac''' ([[International Nonproprietary Name|INN]], or '''biphenylylacetic acid''') is a [[topical]] medicine, belonging to the family of medicines known as [[nonsteroidal anti-inflammatory drug]]s (NSAIDs) of the arylacetic acid (not arylpropionic acid) class, which is used to treat muscle inflammation and [[arthritis]].
