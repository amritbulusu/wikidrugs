'''5-EAPB''' ('''1-(benzofuran-5-yl)-''N''-ethylpropan-2-amine''') is an [[entactogenic]] [[Substituted amphetamine|amphetamine]] which is structurally related to [[5-MAPB]] and [[5-APB]]. It might be predicted to show similar effects to these drugs in humans, but the pharmacology of 5-EAPB remains unstudied as of 2013.
