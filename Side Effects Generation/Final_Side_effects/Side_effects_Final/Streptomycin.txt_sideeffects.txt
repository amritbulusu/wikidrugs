Common side effects include [[vertigo|feeling like the world is spinning]], vomiting, numbness of the face, fever, and rash.<ref name=AHFS2016/> Use during [[pregnancy]] may result in permanent [[deafness]] in the baby.<ref name=AHFS2016/> Use appears to be safe while [[breastfeeding]].<ref name=WHO2008/> It is not recommended in people with [[myasthenia gravis]].<ref name=WHO2008/> Streptomycin is in the [[aminoglycoside]] class of medication.<ref name=AHFS2016/> It works by blocking the ability of [[30S ribosomal subunits]] to make proteins which results in [[bactericidal|bacterial death]].<ref name=AHFS2016/>
