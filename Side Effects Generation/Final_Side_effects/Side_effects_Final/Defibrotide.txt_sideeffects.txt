There is a strong risk of hemorrhage and some people have had hypersensitivity reactions to defibrotide.<ref name=UKlabel/><ref name=USlabel/>

 


 
Common adverse effects, occurring in between 1 and 10% of people, included impaired blood clotting, [[cerebral hemorrhage]], bleeding in the eyes, low blood pressure, bleeding lungs, bleeding stomach or intestines, vomiting, blood in the urine, and bleeding at catheterization sites.<ref name=UKlabel/>

 


 
Other side effects have included diarrhea, nosebleeds, sepsis, [[graft vs host disease]], pneumonia, and infections.<ref name=USlabel/>
