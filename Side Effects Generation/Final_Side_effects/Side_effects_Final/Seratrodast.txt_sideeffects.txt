The most frequently observed (0.1 to 5%) adverse reactions include [[elevated transaminases]], nausea, loss of appetite, stomach discomfort, abdominal pain, diarrhea, constipation, dry mouth, taste disturbance, drowsiness, headache, dizziness, palpitations and malaise.<ref name="Bronica2" /> Less than 0.1% of patients experienced vomiting, thrombocytopenia, epistaxis, bleeding tendency, insomnia, tremor, numbness, hot flushes and edema.<ref name="Bronica2" /> All the adverse reactions reported were of mild to moderate severity, and resolved when the drug was discontinued.<ref name="Bronica2" />
