Lymecycline's side effects can include [[rash]], [[headache]], [[Diarrhea|diarrhoea]], [[ulcerative colitis]], [[nausea]], [[vomiting]], [[dermatitis]], [[Aphasia|dysphasia]], inflammation of the liver, [[Hypersensitive response|hypersensitive reactions]], and [[Vision disorder|visual disturbances]]. When taken for a long period of time, it can cause [[reflux oesophagitis]].<ref>{{cite web|last=Dr Wang|first=Peter|title=Side effects of Tetralysal|url=http://www.steadyhealth.com/about/side_effects_of_tetralysal.html|accessdate=23 March 2011}}</ref>
