Adverse effects are comparable to other statins. Common are [[nausea]], [[indigestion]], [[insomnia]] and headache. [[Myalgia]] (muscle pain), and rarely [[rhabdomyolysis]], characteristic side effects for statins, can also occur.<ref name="Dinnendahl">{{cite journal|title=Arzneistoff-Profile|veditors=Dinnendahl, V, Fricke, U|publisher=Govi Pharmazeutischer Verlag|location=Eschborn, Germany|date=2012|edition=26|volume=2|isbn=978-3-7741-9846-3|language=German}}</ref>
