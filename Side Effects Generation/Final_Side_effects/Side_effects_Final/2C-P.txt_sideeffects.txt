2C-P produces intense [[Psychedelics, dissociatives and deliriants|hallucinogenic]], [[psychedelic drug|psychedelic]], and [[entheogen]]ic effects including open eye visualizations and [[Closed-eye hallucinations|closed-eye visualizations]].<ref name="pihkal"/> It can have a very slow onset if ingested, and peak effects reportedly do not occur for 3 to 5 hours.<ref name="pihkal"/> The peak lasts for five to ten hours, with the overall experience lasting up to 20 hours.
