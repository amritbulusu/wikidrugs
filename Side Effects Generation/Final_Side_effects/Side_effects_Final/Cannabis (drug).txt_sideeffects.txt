Short-term use increases the risk of both minor and major adverse effects.<ref name=JAMA2015/> Common side effects include dizziness, feeling tired, vomiting, and hallucinations.<ref name=JAMA2015/> [[Long-term effects of cannabis]] are not clear.<ref name=JAMA2015/> Concerns include [[Cannabis and memory|memory and cognition problems]], risk of addiction, [[schizophrenia]] in young people, and the risk of children taking it by accident.<ref name=Borgelt2013/>
