Little is known about the subjective effects of 5-MeO-DPT, but the nature of the compound is probably comparable to [[5-MeO-DiPT]], [[5-MeO-DMT]], or [[Dipropyltryptamine|DPT]], which are also [[psychedelic drugs|psychedelic]] tryptamines/indoles. However, the duration of the above-mentioned drugs vary considerably.
