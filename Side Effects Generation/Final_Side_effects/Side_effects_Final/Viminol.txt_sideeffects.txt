Side effects are similar to other opioids, and can include:{{medcn|date=April 2017}}

 


 
* [[Itching]]

 
* [[Nausea]]

 
* [[Sedation]]

 
* [[Respiratory depression]] - can be potentially life-threatening

 


 
However, since viminol is supplied as a racemic mixture of agonist and antagonist isomers, the [[abuse potential]] and respiratory depression tends to be less than that of Î¼-opioid full agonist drugs.{{medcn|date=April 2017}}
