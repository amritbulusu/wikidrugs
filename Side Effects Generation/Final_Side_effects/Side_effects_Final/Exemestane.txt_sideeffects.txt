The most common side effects (more than 10% of patients) are [[hot flash]]es and sweating, which are typical of estrogen deficiency as caused by exemestane, and also [[insomnia]], headache, and [[joint pain]]. Nausea and [[fatigue (medical)|fatigue]] are mainly observed in patients with advanced breast cancer.<ref name="Austria-Codex" /><ref name="Drugs.com">Drugs.com: {{drugs.com|monograph|exemestane}} on exemestane.</ref>

 


 
An occasional decrease in lymphocytes has been observed in approximately 20% of patients receiving Aromasin, particularly in patients with pre-existing lymphopenia. <ref>https://www.medicines.org.uk/emc/medicine/2484</ref>
