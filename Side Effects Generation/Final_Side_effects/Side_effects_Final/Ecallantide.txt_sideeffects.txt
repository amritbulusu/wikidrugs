The most common adverse effects are headache, [[nausea]], [[Fatigue (medical)|fatigue]] and [[diarrhea]]. Less common, but observed in more than 5% of patients in clinical trials, are [[respiratory tract infection]]s, fever, vomiting, itching and upper [[abdominal pain]]. Up to 4% of patients showed [[anaphylaxis]], which led to a [[black box warning]] in the US.<ref name="Dyax">{{cite web |author=Dyax Corp. |year=2009 |title=Full prescibing information Kalbitor |url=http://www.kalbitor.com/pdf/KalbitorFullPrescribingInformation.pdf |accessdate=2010-05-02}}</ref>
