Users report a feeling a sense of [[euphoria]] and energy. The effect has been most closely compared with those of [[MDMA|ecstasy]]. Adverse effects include loss of appetite, jaw tension, [[hallucination]]s, [[paranoia]], [[tachycardia]], [[cardiac arrhythmia]]s, [[insomnia]], [[anxiety disorder]], [[panic disorder]], [[psychotic depression]], [[substance-induced psychosis]], [[stimulant psychosis]], and [[depersonalization disorder]] .
