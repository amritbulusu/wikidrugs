Common side effects when use for cancer include [[nose bleeds]], headache, high blood pressure, and rash.<ref name=AHFS2016/> Other severe side effects include [[gastrointestinal perforation]], bleeding, [[allergic reactions]], [[blood clots]], and an increased risk of infection.<ref name=AHFS2016/> When used for eye disease side effects can include [[vision loss]] and [[retinal detachment]].<ref name=AHFS2016/> Bevacizumab is in the [[angiogenesis inhibitor]] and [[monoclonal antibody]] families of medication.<ref name=AHFS2016/> It works by slowing the [[angiogenesis|growth of new blood vessels]].<ref name=AHFS2016/>
