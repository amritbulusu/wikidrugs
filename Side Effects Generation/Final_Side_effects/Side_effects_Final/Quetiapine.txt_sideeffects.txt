Common side effects include sleepiness, [[constipation]], weight gain, and [[dry mouth]].<ref name=AHFS2017/> Other side effects include [[orthostatic hypotension|low blood pressure with standing]], [[seizures]], [[priapism|prolonged erection]], [[high blood sugar]], and [[neuroleptic malignant syndrome]].<ref name=AHFS2017/> In elderly people with [[dementia]] its use increases the risk of death.<ref name=AHFS2017/> Use during the later part of [[pregnancy]] may result in a [[movement disorder]] in the baby for a period of time after birth.<ref name=AHFS2017/> Quetiapine is believed to work by blocking a number of receptors including [[serotonin receptor|serotonin]] and [[dopamine receptors]].<ref name=AHFS2017/>
