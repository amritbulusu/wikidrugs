Common adverse effects of the eye formulation include conjunctival [[hemorrhage]], eye pain, cataract, [[vitreous detachment]], [[floater]]s, and [[ocular hypertension]].<ref name="Eylea-Info" />

 


 
Zaltrap has adverse effects typical of anti-cancer drugs, such as reduced blood cell count ([[leukopenia]], [[neutropenia]], [[thrombocytopenia]]), gastrointestinal disorders like diarrhoea and abdominal pain, and fatigue. Another common effect is [[hypertension]] (increased blood pressure).<ref name="Zaltrap-Info" />
