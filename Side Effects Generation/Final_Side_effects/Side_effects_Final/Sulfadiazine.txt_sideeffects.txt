Common side effects include nausea, diarrhea, headache, fever, rash, depression, and [[pancreatitis]].<ref name=AHFS2016/> It should not be used in people who have severe liver problems, kidney problems, or [[porphyria]].<ref name=WHO2008/> If used during [[pregnancy]] it may increase the risk of [[kernicterus]] in the baby.<ref name=AHFS2016/> While the company that makes it does not recommend use during [[breastfeeding]], use is believed to be safe if the baby is otherwise healthy.<ref name=pregnancy/> It is in the [[Sulfonamide (medicine)|sulfonamide]] class of medications.<ref name=AHFS2016/>
