Liothyronine may cause a number of side effects, mostly similar to symptoms of [[hyperthyroidism]], which include:<ref>MedlinePlus. [http://www.nlm.nih.gov/medlineplus/druginfo/medmaster/a682462.html "Liothyronine."] Last accessed July 14, 2007.</ref>

 
* weight loss

 
* tremor

 
* headache

 
* upset stomach

 
* vomiting

 
* diarrhea

 
* stomach cramps

 
* nervousness

 
* irritability

 
* insomnia

 
* excessive sweating

 
* increased appetite

 
* fever

 
* changes in menstrual cycle

 
* sensitivity to heat
