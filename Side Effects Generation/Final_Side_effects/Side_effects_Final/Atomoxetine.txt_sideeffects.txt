'''Very common (>10% incidence) adverse effects include:'''

 
* Nausea (26%)

 
* [[Xerostomia]] (Dry mouth) (20%)

 
* Appetite loss (16%)

 
* Insomnia (15%)

 
* Fatigue (10%)

 
* Headache

 
* Cough
