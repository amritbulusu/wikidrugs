Side effects of ergotamine include nausea and vomiting. At higher doses, it can cause raised arterial [[blood pressure]], [[vasoconstriction]] (including [[coronary vasospasm]]) and [[bradycardia]] or [[tachycardia]]. Severe vasoconstriction may cause symptoms of [[intermittent claudication]].<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.drugs.com/pro/medihaler-ergotamine.html|title=Medihaler Ergotamine|website=[[drugs.com]]|accessdate=2016-05-20}}</ref>
